## 문제
$\int_{0}^{\ln 3} e^{x+3} dx$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{e^{3}}{2}$   ② $e^{3}$   ③ $\frac{3}{2} e^{3}$   ④ $2e^{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{5}{2} e^{3}$

## 풀이
[정답] ④ 

$$ \int_{0}^{\ln 3} e^{x+3} dx $$ 
$$ = \int_{0}^{\ln 3} e^{3} e^{x} dx $$ 
$$ = e^{3} [ e^{x} ]_{0}^{\ln 3} $$ 
$$ = e^{3} ( e^{\ln 3} - e^{0} ) $$ 
$$ = e^{3} ( 3 - 1 ) $$ 
$$ = 2e^{3} $$
