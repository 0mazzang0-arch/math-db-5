## 문제
$\lim_{x \to 2005} \frac{2007(2005-x)}{\sqrt{2006-x} - \sqrt{x-2004}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $2004$   ② $2005$   ③ $2006$   ④ $2007$   ⑤ $2008$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$ \begin{aligned} (\text{준식}) &= \lim_{x \to 2005} \frac{2007(2005-x)(\sqrt{2006-x} + \sqrt{x-2004})}{(2006-x)-(x-2004)} \\ &= \lim_{x \to 2005} \frac{2007(2005-x)(\sqrt{2006-x} + \sqrt{x-2004})}{2(2005-x)} \\ &= \lim_{x \to 2005} \frac{2007(\sqrt{2006-x} + \sqrt{x-2004})}{2} \\ &= \frac{2007(1+1)}{2} \\ &= 2007 \end{aligned} $$
