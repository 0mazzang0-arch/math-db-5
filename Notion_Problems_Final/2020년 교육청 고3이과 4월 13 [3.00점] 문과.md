## 문제
$\sum_{n=1}^{20} (-1)^{n} n^{2}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $195$   ② $200$   ③ $205$   ④ $210$   ⑤ $215$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$\sum_{n=1}^{20} (-1)^{n} n^{2}$
$= -1^{2} + 2^{2} - 3^{2} + 4^{2} - \cdots - 19^{2} + 20^{2}$
$= \sum_{n=1}^{10} (2n)^{2} - \sum_{n=1}^{10} (2n-1)^{2}$
$= \sum_{n=1}^{10} (4n-1)$
$= 4 \times \frac{10 \times 11}{2} - 10 = 210$

[다른 풀이]
$\sum_{n=1}^{20} (-1)^{n} n^{2}$
$= (-1^{2} + 2^{2}) + (-3^{2} + 4^{2}) + \cdots + (-19^{2} + 20^{2})$
$= (-1+2) \times (1+2) + (-3+4) \times (3+4) + \cdots + (-19+20) \times (19+20)$
$= 1 + 2 + 3 + \cdots + 20$
$= \frac{20 \times 21}{2}$
$= 210$
