## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -3 & 1 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 3 \\ 4 & -1 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $2A + B$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -4 & 5 \\ 8 & 7 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 5 \\ 6 & 7 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -4 & 4 \\ 8 & 3 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 4 \\ 6 & 3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -2 & 5 \\ 6 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ 2A = 2 \left( \begin{array}{rr} -3 & 1 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -6 & 2 \\ 4 & 8 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 3 \\ 4 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로
$$ 2A + B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -6 & 2 \\ 4 & 8 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 3 \\ 4 & -1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -4 & 5 \\ 8 & 7 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 정답은 ①이다.
