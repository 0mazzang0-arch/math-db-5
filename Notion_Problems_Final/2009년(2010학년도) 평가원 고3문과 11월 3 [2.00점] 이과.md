## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{(n+1)(3n-1)}{2n^{2}+1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{3}{2}$   ② $2$   ③ $\frac{5}{2}$   ④ $3$   ⑤ $\frac{7}{2}$

## 풀이
[정답] ①

$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(n+1)(3n-1)}{2n^{2}+1} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3n^{2}+2n-1}{2n^{2}+1} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3 + \frac{2}{n} - \frac{1}{n^{2}}}{2 + \frac{1}{n^{2}}} $$
$$ = \frac{3}{2} $$
