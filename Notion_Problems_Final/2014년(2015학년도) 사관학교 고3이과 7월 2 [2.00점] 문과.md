## 문제
두 행렬 $$ A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -3 & -5 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) $$, $$ B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 5 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$에 대하여 $$ A X = A + B $$를 만족시키는 행렬 $$ X $$의 모든 성분의 합은?

## 보기
① $9$   ② $11$   ③ $13$   ④ $15$   ⑤ $17$

## 풀이
$$ A X = A + B $$에서 
$$ X = A^{-1} ( A + B ) = E + A^{-1} B $$
$$ A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -3 & -5 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) $$, $$ B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 5 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$이므로 
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 5 \\ -2 & -3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 5 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 20 \\ -2 & -13 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 20 \\ -2 & -12 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 행렬 $$ X $$의 모든 성분의 합은 
$$ 3 + 20 + ( -2 ) + ( -12 ) = 9 $$
[정답] ①
