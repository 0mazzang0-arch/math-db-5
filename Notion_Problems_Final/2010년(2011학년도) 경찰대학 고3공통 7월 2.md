## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A$ 에 대하여 두 연립방정식 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) $$
와 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} y \\ x \end{array} \right) $$
의 해가 모두 무수히 많을 때, $A$ 의 모든 성분의 합은?

## 보기
① $-2$   ② $-1$   ③ $0$   ④ $1$   ⑤ $2$

## 풀이
[정답] ⑤ 

$$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) $$
라 하면 

$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) \Longleftrightarrow \left( \begin{array}{cc} a - 1 & b \\ c & d - 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} y \\ x \end{array} \right) \Longleftrightarrow \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b - 1 \\ c - 1 & d \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$ 
주어진 두 연립방정식의 해가 무수히 많으므로 두 행렬 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a - 1 & b \\ c & d - 1 \end{array} \right) , \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b - 1 \\ c - 1 & d \end{array} \right) $$ 
의 역행렬이 모두 존재하지 않는다. 

$$ (a - 1)(d - 1) - bc = 0 $$
즉, $\cdots \cdots$ ㉠ 

$$ ad - (b - 1)(c - 1) = 0 $$ 
$\cdots \cdots$ ㉡ 

㉡ $-$ ㉠ : 
$$ a + b + c + d - 2 = 0 $$ 

$$ \therefore a + b + c + d = 2 $$
