## 문제
$\sum_{k=1}^{5}(k+1)^{2}-\sum_{k=1}^{5}(k^{2}+k)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $12$   ② $14$   ③ $16$   ④ $18$   ⑤ $20$

## 풀이
$\sum_{k=1}^{5}(k+1)^{2}-\sum_{k=1}^{5}(k^{2}+k)$
$= \sum_{k=1}^{5}\{(k+1)^{2}-(k^{2}+k)\}$
$= \sum_{k=1}^{5}(k^{2}+2k+1-k^{2}-k)$
$= \sum_{k=1}^{5}(k+1)$
$= \sum_{k=1}^{5}k+\sum_{k=1}^{5}1$
$= \frac{5 \times 6}{2} + 5$
$= 15 + 5 = 20$

정답: ⑤
