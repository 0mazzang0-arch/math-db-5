## 문제
$6^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{5}{3}} \times 3^{-\frac{1}{3}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $6$   ② $12$   ③ $24$   ④ $36$   ⑤ $54$

## 풀이
$6^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{5}{3}} \times 3^{-\frac{1}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 3^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{5}{3}} \times 3^{-\frac{1}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{5}{3}} \times 3^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 3^{-\frac{1}{3}}$
$= 2^{3} \times 3^{1} = 24$
