## 문제
$\cos A = \frac{1}{4}$ 일 때, $\sin^2 \frac{A}{2} + \cos 2A$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-\frac{1}{2}$   ② $-\frac{1}{3}$   ③ $-\frac{1}{4}$   ④ $-\frac{1}{5}$   ⑤ $-\frac{1}{6}$

## 풀이
$\sin^{2} \frac{A}{2} + \cos 2A$
$= \frac{1-\cos A}{2} + 2\cos^{2} A - 1$
$\cos A = \frac{1}{4}$ 을 대입하면
$= \frac{1-\frac{1}{4}}{2} + 2\left(\frac{1}{4}\right)^{2} - 1$
$= \frac{\frac{3}{4}}{2} + 2\left(\frac{1}{16}\right) - 1$
$= \frac{3}{8} + \frac{1}{8} - 1$
$= \frac{4}{8} - 1$
$= \frac{1}{2} - 1 = -\frac{1}{2}$
