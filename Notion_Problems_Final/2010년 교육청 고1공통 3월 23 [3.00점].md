## 문제
다음의 값을 구하시오.

$$\frac{2010^{2} - 2002 \times 2018}{2011^{2} - 2009 \times 2013}$$

## 풀이
[정답] $16$

[해설] 
$$\frac{2010^{2} - 2002 \times 2018}{2011^{2} - 2009 \times 2013}$$
$$= \frac{2010^{2} - (2010 - 8) \times (2010 + 8)}{2011^{2} - (2011 - 2) \times (2011 + 2)}$$
$$= \frac{2010^{2} - 2010^{2} + 64}{2011^{2} - 2011^{2} + 4}$$
$$= \frac{64}{4}$$
$$= 16$$

[다른풀이] 
$2010 = x$, $2011 = y$라 하면 
$$\frac{2010^{2} - 2002 \times 2018}{2011^{2} - 2009 \times 2013} = \frac{x^{2} - (x - 8) \times (x + 8)}{y^{2} - (y - 2) \times (y + 2)}$$
$$= \frac{x^{2} - x^{2} + 64}{y^{2} - y^{2} + 4}$$
$$= \frac{64}{4}$$
$$= 16$$
