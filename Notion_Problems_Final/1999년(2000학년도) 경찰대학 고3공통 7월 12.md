## 문제
무한급수 $\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{2^{n}}{2^{2n+1}-3 \cdot 2^{n}+1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5}$   ② $\frac{1}{4}$   ③ $\frac{1}{3}$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=2}^{n} \frac{2^{k}}{2^{2k+1}-3 \cdot 2^{k}+1}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=2}^{n} \frac{2^{k}}{\left( 2^{k+1}-1 \right) \left( 2^{k}-1 \right)}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=2}^{n} \left( \frac{1}{2^{k}-1} - \frac{1}{2^{k+1}-1} \right)$
이므로
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \left\{ \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{7} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{7} - \frac{1}{15} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{2^{n}-1} - \frac{1}{2^{n+1}-1} \right) \right\}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{2^{n+1}-1} \right) = \frac{1}{3}$
