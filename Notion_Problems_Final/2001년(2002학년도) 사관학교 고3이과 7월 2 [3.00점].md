## 문제
일차변환 $f$는 점 $(2, 1)$을 점 $(3, 2)$로 옮기고, 합성변환 $f \circ f$는 점 $(2, 1)$을 점 $(1, -2)$로 옮긴다. 일차변환 $f$에 의하여 점 $(-2, -4)$로 옮겨지는 점은?

## 보기
① $(1, 0)$   ② $(-1, 0)$   ③ $(1, 1)$   ④ $(-1, 1)$   ⑤ $(-1, -1)$

## 풀이
$f((2, 1)) = (3, 2)$
$(f \circ f)((2, 1)) = f(f((2, 1))) = f((3, 2)) = (1, -2)$

일차변환 $f$의 행렬을 $$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) $$라 두면

$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 3 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \right) \Leftrightarrow \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -3 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ \therefore \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & -7 \\ 6 & -10 \end{array} \right) $$

그러므로

$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & -7 \\ 6 & -10 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) $$

$$ \therefore \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = -\frac{1}{8} \left( \begin{array}{cc} -10 & 7 \\ -6 & 5 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -4 \end{array} \right) = -\frac{1}{8} \left( \begin{array}{c} -8 \\ -8 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) $$

따라서 구하는 점의 좌표는 $(1, 1)$이다.

정답: ③
