## 문제
$(\sqrt[3]{4})^{2} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $4$   ④ $8$   ⑤ $16$

## 풀이
$$(\sqrt[3]{4})^{2} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}}$$
$$= (2^{\frac{2}{3}})^{2} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{4}{3} + \frac{2}{3}}$$
$$= 2^{2} = 4$$

정답: ③
