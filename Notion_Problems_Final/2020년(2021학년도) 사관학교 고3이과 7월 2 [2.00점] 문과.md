## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}-n}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5}$   ② $\frac{2}{5}$   ③ $\frac{3}{5}$   ④ $\frac{4}{5}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
정답: ②
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}-n} = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}+n}{(\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}-n)(\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}+n)}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}+n}{(n^{2}+5n)-n^{2}}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n^{2}+5n}+n}{5n}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{1+\frac{5}{n}}+1}{5}$
$= \frac{1+1}{5} = \frac{2}{5}$
