## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 2} \frac{1}{x - 2} \left( \frac{1}{x + 1} - \frac{1}{3} \right)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-\frac{1}{9}$   ② $-\frac{1}{6}$   ③ $-\frac{1}{4}$   ④ $-\frac{1}{3}$   ⑤ $-\frac{1}{2}$

## 풀이
$$ \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{1}{x - 2} \left( \frac{1}{x + 1} - \frac{1}{3} \right) = \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{1}{x - 2} \left( \frac{3 - (x + 1)}{3(x + 1)} \right) $$
$$ = \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{-(x - 2)}{3(x - 2)(x + 1)} $$
$$ = \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{-1}{3(x + 1)} = -\frac{1}{9} $$
따라서 정답은 ① 이다.
