## 문제
$\sum_{k=1}^{7} \frac{1}{(k+1)(k+2)}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{6}$   ② $\frac{2}{9}$   ③ $\frac{5}{18}$   ④ $\frac{1}{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{7}{18}$

## 풀이
[정답] ⑤

$$ \sum_{k=1}^{7} \frac{1}{(k+1)(k+2)} = \sum_{k=1}^{7} \left( \frac{1}{k+1} - \frac{1}{k+2} \right) $$

$$ = \left( \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{3} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{4} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{8} - \frac{1}{9} \right) $$

$$ = \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{9} = \frac{7}{18} $$
