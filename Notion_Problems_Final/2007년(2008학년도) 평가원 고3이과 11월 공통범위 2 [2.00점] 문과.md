## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 $A = 2B - X$를 만족시키는 행렬 $X$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & -1 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & 1 \\ -2 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
정답 ①

$A = 2B - X$를 $X$에 관하여 정리하면
$$ X = 2B - A $$
$$ X = 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 0 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \right) $$
