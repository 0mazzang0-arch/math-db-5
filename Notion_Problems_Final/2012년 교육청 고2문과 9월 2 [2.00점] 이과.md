## 문제
두 행렬 $X = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & -3 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $Y = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 $X - 2Y$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & -3 \\ 8 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & -4 \\ 8 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & -4 \\ 10 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -3 \\ 10 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -4 \\ 10 & -1 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & -3 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) - 2 \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & -3 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 0 \\ -4 & 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & -3 \\ 8 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 정답은 ①이다.
