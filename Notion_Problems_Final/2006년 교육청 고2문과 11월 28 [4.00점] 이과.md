## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 $A + A^{2} + A^{3} + \cdots + A^{100} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$ 일 때, $a + b + c + d$ 의 값을 구하시오. (단, $A^{n+1} = A^{n} A$)

## 보기

## 풀이
$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$
$A^{2} = A A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -2 & -1 \end{array} \right)$
$A^{3} = A^{2} A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ -2 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 3 \\ -3 & -2 \end{array} \right)$
$\vdots$
$A^{n} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} n+1 & n \\ -n & -n+1 \end{array} \right)$
따라서 $A^{n}$ 의 성분의 합은 항상 $2$ 이다.
$A + A^{2} + \cdots + A^{100} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$ 에서
$\therefore a + b + c + d = 2 \times 100 = 200$

정답: $200$
