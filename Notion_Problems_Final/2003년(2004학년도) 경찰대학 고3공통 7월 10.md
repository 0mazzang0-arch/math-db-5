## 문제
행렬 $$ B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$과 단위행렬 $$ E $$에 대하여
$$ A_{0} = E $$, $$ A_{n} = B^{n} A_{n-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a_{n} & b_{n} \\ c_{n} & d_{n} \end{array} \right) $$ ($$ n = 1, 2, \dots $$)일 때, $$ a_{n} + b_{n} + c_{n} + d_{n} \le 2004 $$를 만족시키는 최대의 자연수 $$ n $$은?

## 보기
① $29$   ② $30$   ③ $31$   ④ $32$   ⑤ $33$

## 풀이
[정답] ③

$$ B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ B^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 8 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ B^{3} = B^{2} \cdot B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 8 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 12 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \vdots $$
$$ B^{n} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4n & 1 \end{array} \right) \cdots \text{①} $$

주어진 점화식에 $$ n = 1, 2, 3, \dots $$를 차례로 대입하면
$$ n = 1 $$일 때 : $$ A_{1} = B \cdot E = B $$
$$ n = 2 $$일 때 : $$ A_{2} = B^{2} \cdot A_{1} = B^{2} \cdot B = B^{3} $$
$$ n = 3 $$일 때 : $$ A_{3} = B^{3} \cdot A_{2} = B^{3} \cdot B^{3} = B^{6} $$
$$ \vdots $$
$$ n = n $$일 때 : $$ A_{n} = B^{\frac{1}{2} n(n+1)} $$

따라서, 식 ①에 의해
$$ \therefore \left( \begin{array}{cc} a_{n} & b_{n} \\ c_{n} & d_{n} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 \cdot \frac{n(n+1)}{2} & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 2n(n+1) & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

그러므로
$$ a_{n} + b_{n} + c_{n} + d_{n} = 1 + 0 + 2n(n+1) + 1 = 2(n^{2} + n + 1) \le 2004 $$
$$ n^{2} + n + 1 \le 1002 $$
$$ n(n + 1) \le 1001 $$

이를 만족하는 최대 자연수 $$ n $$은 $$ 31 $$이다.
(참고: $$ 31 \times 32 = 992 $$, $$ 32 \times 33 = 1056 $$)
