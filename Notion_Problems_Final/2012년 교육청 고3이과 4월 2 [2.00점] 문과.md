## 문제
두 행렬 
$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$, 
$B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 
$2 A + X = B$를 만족시키는 행렬 $X$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 1 \\ -3 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 3 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 2 \\ -1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 4 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$2 A + X = B$를 $X$에 관하여 정리하면
$X = B - 2 A$이다.

주어진 행렬 $A$와 $B$를 대입하면
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) - 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & 0 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 0 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 - (-2) & 2 - 0 \\ 1 - 2 & 3 - 4 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 2 \\ -1 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$

따라서 정답은 ④이다.
