## 문제
첫째항이 $1$이고 공차가 $3$인 등차수열 $\{a_{n}\}$에 대하여 $\sum_{n=1}^{10} \frac{1}{a_{2n-1} a_{2n+1}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{10}{61}$  ② $\frac{12}{61}$  ③ $\frac{14}{61}$  ④ $\frac{16}{61}$  ⑤ $\frac{18}{61}$

## 풀이
$a_{n}=3n-2$ 이므로 

$\sum_{n=1}^{10} \frac{1}{a_{2n-1} a_{2n+1}}$ 

$= \sum_{n=1}^{10} \frac{1}{a_{2n+1}-a_{2n-1}} \left( \frac{1}{a_{2n-1}}-\frac{1}{a_{2n+1}} \right)$ 

$= \frac{1}{6} \left\{ \left( \frac{1}{a_{1}}-\frac{1}{a_{3}} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{a_{3}}-\frac{1}{a_{5}} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{a_{19}}-\frac{1}{a_{21}} \right) \right\}$ 

$= \frac{1}{6} \times \left( 1-\frac{1}{61} \right) = \frac{10}{61}$

정답: ①
