## 문제
함수 $f(x) = \cos x$ 에 대하여
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{k \pi}{n^{2}} f \left( \frac{\pi}{2} + \frac{k \pi}{n} \right)$$
의 값은?

## 보기
① $-\frac{5}{2}$   ② $-2$   ③ $-\frac{3}{2}$   ④ $-1$   ⑤ $-\frac{1}{2}$

## 풀이
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{k \pi}{n^{2}} f \left( \frac{\pi}{2} + \frac{k \pi}{n} \right)$$
$$= \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} x f \left( \frac{\pi}{2} + x \right) dx$$
$$= \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} x \cos \left( \frac{\pi}{2} + x \right) dx$$
$$= \frac{1}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi} x (-\sin x) dx$$
$$= \frac{1}{\pi} \left( [x \cos x]_{0}^{\pi} - \int_{0}^{\pi} \cos x dx \right)$$
$$= \frac{1}{\pi} (-\pi - [\sin x]_{0}^{\pi})$$
$$= \frac{1}{\pi} \times (-\pi)$$
$$= -1$$

정답: ④
