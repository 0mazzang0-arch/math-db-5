## 문제
$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \sin 2x dx$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{2}$   ② $1$   ③ $\frac{3}{2}$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $\frac{5}{2}$

## 풀이
$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}} \sin 2x dx = \left[ -\frac{1}{2} \cos 2x \right]_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}} = -\frac{1}{2} (\cos \frac{\pi}{2} - \cos 0) = -\frac{1}{2} (0 - 1) = \frac{1}{2}$

정답: ①
