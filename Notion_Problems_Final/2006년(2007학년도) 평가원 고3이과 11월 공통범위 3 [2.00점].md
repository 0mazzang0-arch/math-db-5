## 문제
$\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 - 1}{\sqrt{x+3} - 2}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $7$   ② $8$   ③ $9$   ④ $10$   ⑤ $11$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$\lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^2 - 1}{\sqrt{x+3} - 2}$ 
$= \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{(x^2 - 1)(\sqrt{x+3} + 2)}{(\sqrt{x+3} - 2)(\sqrt{x+3} + 2)}$ 
$= \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{(x-1)(x+1)(\sqrt{x+3} + 2)}{(x+3) - 4}$
$= \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{(x-1)(x+1)(\sqrt{x+3} + 2)}{x-1}$ 
$= \lim_{x \to 1} (x+1)(\sqrt{x+3} + 2)$ 
$= (1+1)(\sqrt{1+3} + 2) = 2 \times 4 = 8$
