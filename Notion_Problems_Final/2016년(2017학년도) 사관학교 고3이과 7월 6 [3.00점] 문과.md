## 문제
$\lim_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} (1 - \cos x)^{\sec x}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{e^{2}}$   ② $\frac{1}{e}$   ③ $1$   ④ $e$   ⑤ $e^{2}$

## 풀이
$\frac{\pi}{2} - x = \theta$로 치환하면 $x \to \frac{\pi}{2}$일 때 $\theta \to 0$이다.

$\lim_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} (1 - \cos x)^{\sec x}$
$= \lim_{\theta \to 0} \{ 1 - \cos ( \frac{\pi}{2} - \theta ) \}^{\frac{1}{\cos ( \frac{\pi}{2} - \theta )}}$
$= \lim_{\theta \to 0} ( 1 - \sin \theta )^{\frac{1}{\sin \theta}}$
$= \lim_{\theta \to 0} \{ ( 1 - \sin \theta )^{\frac{1}{-\sin \theta}} \}^{-1}$
$= e^{-1}$
$= \frac{1}{e}$

정답: ②
