## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & 3 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 두 수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$, $\{ b_{n} \}$이
$$ a_{n} A + b_{n} B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 n - 3 \cdot 2^{n} & 12 n + 3 \cdot 2^{n} \\ 3 n + 2^{n} & 2^{n+1} \end{array} \right) $$
($n = 1, 2, 3, \dots$)을 만족시킬 때, $\sum_{k=1}^{10} a_{k}$의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기

## 풀이
$$ a_{n} A + b_{n} B $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 a_{n} & 4 a_{n} \\ a_{n} & 0 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 b_{n} & 3 b_{n} \\ b_{n} & 2 b_{n} \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 n - 3 \cdot 2^{n} & 12 n + 3 \cdot 2^{n} \\ 3 n + 2^{n} & 2^{n+1} \end{array} \right) $$
이므로
$2 b_{n} = 2^{n+1} \Rightarrow b_{n} = 2^{n}$,
$a_{n} + b_{n} = 3 n + 2^{n} \Rightarrow a_{n} = 3 n$이다.
$\therefore \sum_{k=1}^{10} a_{k} = \sum_{k=1}^{10} 3 k = 3 \cdot \frac{10 \cdot 11}{2} = 165$

정답: $165$
