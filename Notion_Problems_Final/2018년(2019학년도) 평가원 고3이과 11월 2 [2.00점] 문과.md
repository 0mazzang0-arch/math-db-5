## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{x^{2} + 5x}{\ln (1 + 3x)}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{7}{3}$   ② $2$   ③ $\frac{5}{3}$   ④ $\frac{4}{3}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
정답: ③

$$ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x^{2} + 5x}{\ln (1 + 3x)} $$
$$ = \lim_{x \to 0} \left( \frac{3x}{\ln (1 + 3x)} \times \frac{x + 5}{3} \right) $$
$$ = 1 \times \frac{5}{3} = \frac{5}{3} $$
