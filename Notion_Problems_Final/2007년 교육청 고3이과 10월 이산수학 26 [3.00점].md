## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \left( n \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{n}} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x+1}} dx \right)$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
[정답] ①

$$\int_{0}^{\frac{1}{n}} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x+1}} dx = \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{n}} (x+1)^{-\frac{1}{2}} dx$$ 
$$= \frac{2(\sqrt{n+1} - \sqrt{n})}{\sqrt{n}}$$ 
$\therefore$ 
$$\lim_{n \to \infty} \left( n \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{n}} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x+1}} dx \right)$$ 
$$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2 \sqrt{n}}{\sqrt{n+1} + \sqrt{n}} = 1$$
