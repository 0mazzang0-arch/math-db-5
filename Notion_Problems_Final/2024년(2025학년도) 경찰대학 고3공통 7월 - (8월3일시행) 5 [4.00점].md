## 문제
$1^{3} - 2^{3} + 3^{3} - 4^{3} + \cdots + 19^{3}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $3300$   ② $3400$   ③ $3500$   ④ $3600$   ⑤ $3700$

## 풀이
$$1^{3} - 2^{3} + 3^{3} - 4^{3} + \cdots + 19^{3}$$
$$= (1^{3} + 3^{3} + \cdots + 19^{3}) - (2^{3} + 4^{3} + \cdots + 18^{3})$$
$$= \sum_{k=1}^{10} (2k - 1)^{3} - \sum_{k=1}^{9} (2k)^{3}$$
$$= \sum_{k=1}^{10} (8k^{3} - 12k^{2} + 6k - 1) - 8 \sum_{k=1}^{9} k^{3}$$
$$= 8 \times 10^{3} + \sum_{k=1}^{10} (-12k^{2} + 6k - 1)$$
$$= 8000 - 12 \times \frac{10 \times 11 \times 21}{6} + 6 \times \frac{10 \times 11}{2} - 10$$
$$= 8000 - 4620 + 330 - 10$$
$$= 3700$$

정답: ⑤
