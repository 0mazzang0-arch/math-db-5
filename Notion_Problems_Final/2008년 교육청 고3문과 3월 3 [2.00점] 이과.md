## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{2n+1} + \sqrt{2n-1}}{\sqrt{n+1} + \sqrt{n-1}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2 \sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{2n+1} + \sqrt{2n-1}}{\sqrt{n+1} + \sqrt{n-1}}$ 
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{2+ \frac{1}{n}} + \sqrt{2- \frac{1}{n}}}{\sqrt{1+ \frac{1}{n}} + \sqrt{1- \frac{1}{n}}} = \frac{2\sqrt{2}}{2} = \sqrt{2}$
