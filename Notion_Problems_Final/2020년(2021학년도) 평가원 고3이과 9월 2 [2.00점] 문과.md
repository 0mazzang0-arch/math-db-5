## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(2n+1)^{2} - (2n-1)^{2}}{2n+5}$의 값은? [$2$점]

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(2n+1)^{2} - (2n-1)^{2}}{2n+5}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(4n) \cdot 2}{2n+5}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{8n}{2n+5}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{8}{2 + \frac{5}{n}}$
$= 4$
