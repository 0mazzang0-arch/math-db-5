## 문제
$A+B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array} \right)$, $AX= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right)$, $BX= \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & -1 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array} \right)$ 를 만족시키는 이차정사각행렬 $X$ 는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 8 & 4 \\ -24 & -12 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 8 & -4 \\ 24 & -12 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -8 & -4 \\ 24 & 12 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 24 & 12 \\ -8 & -4 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 24 & -12 \\ 8 & -4 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ AX+BX=(A+B)X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & -1 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 8 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array} \right) X= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 8 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$
가 된다. 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array} \right)^{-1} = \frac{1}{3 \times 2 - 1 \times 5} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 
$$ \therefore X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 8 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -8 & -4 \\ 24 & 12 \end{array} \right) $$
정답은 ③이다.
