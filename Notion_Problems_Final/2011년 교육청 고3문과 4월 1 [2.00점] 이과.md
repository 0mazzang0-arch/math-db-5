## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $AB + B$ 의 모든 성분의 합은?

## 보기
① $4$   ② $5$   ③ $6$   ④ $7$   ⑤ $8$

## 풀이
$$ AB + B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 1 \\ -4 & -2 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 8 & 1 \\ -2 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 모든 성분의 합은 
$$ 8 + 1 + (-2) + (-1) = 6 $$
정답: ③
