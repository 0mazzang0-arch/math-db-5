## 문제
두 행렬 $X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right)$, $Y = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -4 \\ 5 & -1 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $X^{2}-2 Y$의 $(1, 2)$ 성분을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$$ X^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 7 & 10 \\ 15 & 22 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ X^{2}-2 Y = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 7 & 10 \\ 15 & 22 \end{array} \right) - 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -4 \\ 5 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 18 \\ 5 & 24 \end{array} \right) $$

따라서 행렬 $X^{2}-2 Y$의 $(1, 2)$ 성분은 $18$이다.

정답: $18$
