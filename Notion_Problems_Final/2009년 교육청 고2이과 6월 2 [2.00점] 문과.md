## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $A - A^{-1}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 4 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 역행렬 $A^{-1}$ 을 구하면 다음과 같다.
$$ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{1 \cdot 1 - 0 \cdot 2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

따라서 $A - A^{-1}$ 은 다음과 같다.
$$ A - A^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 4 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
