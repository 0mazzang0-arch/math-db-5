## 문제
$a > 0$, $a \neq 1$ 일 때, $\frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt[3]{a}} \times \sqrt{\sqrt[3]{a}}$ 를 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $\sqrt[6]{a}$   ② $\sqrt[3]{a}$   ③ $\sqrt[5]{a^{2}}$   ④ $\sqrt{a}$   ⑤ $\sqrt[4]{a^{3}}$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$\frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt[3]{a}} \times \sqrt{\sqrt[3]{a}}$
$= \frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt[3]{a}} \times \sqrt[6]{a}$
$= a^{\frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{6}}$
$= a^{\frac{3-2+1}{6}}$
$= a^{\frac{2}{6}}$
$= a^{\frac{1}{3}}$
$= \sqrt[3]{a}$
