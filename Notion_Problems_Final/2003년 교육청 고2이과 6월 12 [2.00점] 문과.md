## 문제
$x$, $y$에 대한 두 연립방정식 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 8 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 7 & 9 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ k \end{array} \right) $$
의 해가 서로 같을 때 실수 $k$의 값은? 

## 보기
① $11$   ② $12$   ③ $13$   ④ $14$   ⑤ $15$

## 풀이
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 8 \end{array} \right) \Leftrightarrow \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right)^{-1} \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 8 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \therefore \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right)^{-1} \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 8 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -5 & 4 \\ 4 & -3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 8 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \therefore \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 7 & 9 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 14 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ k \end{array} \right) $$ 
따라서 
$$ k=14 $$ 

**[다른 풀이]** 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 8 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \Leftrightarrow \begin{cases} 3x+4y=6 & \cdots ㉠ \\ 4x+5y=8 & \cdots ㉡ \end{cases} $$ 

$$ \Leftrightarrow \begin{cases} 3x+4y=6 & \cdots ㉠ \\ 7x+9y=14 & \cdots ㉠+㉡ \end{cases} $$ 

$$ \Leftrightarrow \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 7 & 9 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 6 \\ 14 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \therefore k=14 $$
