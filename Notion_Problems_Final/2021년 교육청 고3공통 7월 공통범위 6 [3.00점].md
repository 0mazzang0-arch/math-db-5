## 문제
$\cos(-\theta) + \sin(\pi + \theta) = \frac{3}{5}$ 일 때, $\sin\theta\cos\theta$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5}$   ② $\frac{6}{25}$   ③ $\frac{7}{25}$   ④ $\frac{8}{25}$   ⑤ $\frac{9}{25}$

## 풀이
$\cos(-\theta) + \sin(\pi + \theta)$ 
$= \cos\theta - \sin\theta$ 
$= \frac{3}{5}$ 

$(\cos\theta - \sin\theta)^{2}$ 
$= \cos^{2}\theta - 2\cos\theta\sin\theta + \sin^{2}\theta$ 
$= 1 - 2\sin\theta\cos\theta$ 

$1 - 2\sin\theta\cos\theta = (\frac{3}{5})^{2}$
$1 - 2\sin\theta\cos\theta = \frac{9}{25}$ 

따라서 
$2\sin\theta\cos\theta = 1 - \frac{9}{25} = \frac{16}{25}$
$\sin\theta\cos\theta = \frac{8}{25}$
