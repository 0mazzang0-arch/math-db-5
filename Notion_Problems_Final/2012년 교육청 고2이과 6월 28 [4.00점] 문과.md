## 문제
첫째항이 양수인 등차수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$에 대하여 
$S_{n}= \sum_{k=1}^{n}(-1)^{k} a_{k}$, $T_{n}= \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{a_{k} a_{k+1}}$이다. 
$S_{10}=25$, $\frac{1}{T_{5}}=\frac{54}{5}$일 때, $a_{6}$의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
등차수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$의 첫째항을 $a$, 공차를 $d$라 하자.

$S_{10}= \sum_{k=1}^{10}(-1)^{k} a_{k}$
$= -a_{1}+a_{2}-a_{3}+a_{4}-a_{5}+a_{6}-a_{7}+a_{8}-a_{9}+a_{10}$
$= (a_{2}-a_{1} )+ (a_{4}-a_{3} )+ (a_{6}-a_{5} )+ (a_{8}-a_{7} )+ (a_{10}-a_{9} )$
$= d+d+d+d+d$
$= 5d$

$S_{10}= 25$이므로 $5d=25 \quad \therefore d=5$

$\frac{1}{a_{k} a_{k+1}}=\frac{1}{a_{k+1}-a_{k}} \left(\frac{1}{a_{k}}-\frac{1}{a_{k+1}} \right)$
그런데 수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$은 공차가 $d=5$인 등차수열이므로 $a_{k+1}-a_{k}=d=5$
즉, $\frac{1}{a_{k+1}-a_{k}}=\frac{1}{5}$
그러므로 $\frac{1}{a_{k} a_{k+1}}=\frac{1}{5} \left(\frac{1}{a_{k}}-\frac{1}{a_{k+1}} \right)$

$T_{5}= \sum_{k=1}^{5} \frac{1}{a_{k} a_{k+1}}$
$= \frac{1}{5} \{ (\frac{1}{a_{1}}-\frac{1}{a_{2}} )+ (\frac{1}{a_{2}}-\frac{1}{a_{3}} )+ (\frac{1}{a_{3}}-\frac{1}{a_{4}} )+ (\frac{1}{a_{4}}-\frac{1}{a_{5}} )+ (\frac{1}{a_{5}}-\frac{1}{a_{6}} ) \}$
$= \frac{1}{5} (\frac{1}{a_{1}}-\frac{1}{a_{6}} )$
$= \frac{1}{5} (\frac{1}{a}-\frac{1}{a+5d} )$
$= \frac{1}{5} (\frac{1}{a}-\frac{1}{a+25} )$
$= \frac{1}{5} \cdot \frac{25}{a(a+25)}$
$= \frac{5}{a(a+25)}$

$\frac{1}{T_{5}}=\frac{54}{5}$이므로
$\frac{a(a+25)}{5}=\frac{54}{5}$
$a(a+25)=54$
$a^{2}+25a-54=0$
$(a+27)(a-2)=0$
$\therefore a=2 (\because a>0)$

따라서 $a_{6}=2+5 \times 5=27$
