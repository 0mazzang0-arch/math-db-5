## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2}{\sqrt{n^{2} + 2n} - \sqrt{n^{2} - 2n}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $\frac{3}{2}$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $2\sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
정답: ①

$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2}{\sqrt{n^2 + 2n} - \sqrt{n^2 - 2n}} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2(\sqrt{n^2 + 2n} + \sqrt{n^2 - 2n})}{4n} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2 \left( \sqrt{\frac{n^2}{n^2} + \frac{2n}{n^2}} + \sqrt{\frac{n^2}{n^2} - \frac{2n}{n^2}} \right)}{\frac{4n}{n}} $$
$$ = \frac{4}{4} = 1 $$
