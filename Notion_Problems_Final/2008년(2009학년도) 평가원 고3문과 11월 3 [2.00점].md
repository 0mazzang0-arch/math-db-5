## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{2}{\sqrt{n^{2} + 2n} - \sqrt{n^{2} + 1}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
[정답] ②

$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2}{\sqrt{n^2 + 2n} - \sqrt{n^2 + 1}} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2(\sqrt{n^2 + 2n} + \sqrt{n^2 + 1})}{(n^2 + 2n) - (n^2 + 1)} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2(\sqrt{n^2 + 2n} + \sqrt{n^2 + 1})}{2n - 1} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2(\sqrt{1 + \frac{2}{n}} + \sqrt{1 + \frac{1}{n^2}})}{2 - \frac{1}{n}} $$
$$ = \frac{2(1 + 1)}{2} $$
$$ = 2 $$
