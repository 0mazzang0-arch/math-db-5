## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여
$$A+B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right), AB+BA = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & -7 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right)$$
가 성립할 때, $A^{2}+B^{2}$은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -5 & -6 \\ 9 & -2 \end{array} \right)$  ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 6 & -4 \end{array} \right)$  ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -5 & -9 \\ 6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$  ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & 1 \\ 6 & -4 \end{array} \right)$  ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & 7 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$(A+B)^{2}=A^{2}+A B+B A+B^{2}$ 이므로
$A^{2}+B^{2}=(A+B)^{2}-(A B+B A)$ 이다. 

따라서,
$$ \begin{aligned} A^{2}+B^{2} &= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & -7 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) \\ &= \left( \begin{array}{cc} -5 & -6 \\ 9 & -2 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & -7 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) \\ &= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 6 & -4 \end{array} \right) \end{aligned} $$

정답: ②
