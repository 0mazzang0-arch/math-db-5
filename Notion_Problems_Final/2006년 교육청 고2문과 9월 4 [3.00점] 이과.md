## 문제
$\cos \theta = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}$ 일 때, 행렬
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & \tan \theta \\ -\tan \theta & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
의 역행렬은? (단, $0 < \theta < \frac{\pi}{2}$)

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\frac{1}{5} \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\frac{1}{5} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $5 \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & 2 \\ 2 & -3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $5 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 4 \\ -4 & 1 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$\tan^{2} \theta = \sec^{2} \theta - 1 = \frac{1}{\cos^{2} \theta} - 1 = 4$
한편 $0 < \theta < \frac{\pi}{2}$ 이므로 $\tan \theta > 0$
$\therefore \tan \theta = 2$
따라서 구하는 역행렬은
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & \tan \theta \\ -\tan \theta & 1 \end{array} \right)^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right)^{-1} = \frac{1}{5} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

[정답] ①
