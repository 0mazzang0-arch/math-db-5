## 문제
$\frac{1}{2-\sqrt{2}}+\frac{1}{2+\sqrt{2}}$을 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $\sqrt{2}$   ② $2$   ③ $2\sqrt{2}$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $4\sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$ \frac{1}{2-\sqrt{2}} + \frac{1}{2+\sqrt{2}} $$
$$ = \frac{(2+\sqrt{2})+(2-\sqrt{2})}{(2-\sqrt{2})(2+\sqrt{2})} $$
$$ = \frac{4}{2^{2}-(\sqrt{2})^{2}} = \frac{4}{4-2} = \frac{4}{2} = 2 $$
