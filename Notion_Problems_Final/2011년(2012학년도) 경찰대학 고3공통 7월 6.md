## 문제
실수 $x$, $y$에 대하여 $\left( \frac{1+i}{1-\sqrt{3}i} \right)^{13} = x+yi$ 가 성립할 때, $|x| + |y|$ 의 값은? (단, $i = \sqrt{-1}$)

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{2^8}$   ② $\frac{1}{2^7}$   ③ $\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2^7}$   ④ $\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2^6}$   ⑤ $\frac{1+\sqrt{3}}{2^6}$

## 풀이
$(1+i)^2 = 2i$,
$(1-\sqrt{3}i)^3 = -8$ 이므로

$\left( \frac{1+i}{1-\sqrt{3}i} \right)^{13}$
$= \left( \frac{1+i}{1-\sqrt{3}i} \right)^{12} \times \left( \frac{1+i}{1-\sqrt{3}i} \right)$
$= \frac{(1+i)^{12}}{(1-\sqrt{3}i)^{12}} \times \left( \frac{1+i}{1-\sqrt{3}i} \right)$
$= \frac{\{(1+i)^{2}\}^{6}}{\{(1-\sqrt{3}i)^{3}\}^{4}} \times \left( \frac{1+i}{1-\sqrt{3}i} \right) \times \frac{1+\sqrt{3}i}{1+\sqrt{3}i}$
$= \frac{(2i)^6}{(-8)^4} \times \frac{1-\sqrt{3} + (1+\sqrt{3})i}{4}$
$= \frac{-2^6}{2^{12}} \times \frac{1-\sqrt{3} + (1+\sqrt{3})i}{4}$
$= \frac{\sqrt{3}-1}{2^8} - \frac{\sqrt{3}+1}{2^8}i$
$= x+yi$

복소수가 서로 같을 조건에 의하여
$x = \frac{\sqrt{3}-1}{2^8}$, $y = -\frac{\sqrt{3}+1}{2^8}$

$\therefore |x| + |y| = \frac{\sqrt{3}-1}{2^8} + \frac{\sqrt{3}+1}{2^8} = \frac{2\sqrt{3}}{2^8} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2^7}$

정답: ③
