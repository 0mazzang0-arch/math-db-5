## 문제
$A+B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} -4 & -1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right) $, $A-B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -3 \\ 1 & 4 \end{array} \right) $를 만족하는 행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여 $A B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) $일 때, $|a d-b c|$의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) $, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & 1 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $이므로
$$ A B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & 1 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ -3 & -2 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 $|a d-b c|=3$이다.

정답: $3$
