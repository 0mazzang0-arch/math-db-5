## 문제
$\sqrt[3]{2} \times \sqrt[6]{16}$ 을 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $2$   ② $4$   ③ $\sqrt{2}$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $2\sqrt[3]{2}$

## 풀이
$\sqrt[3]{2} \times \sqrt[6]{16} = 2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times (2^{4})^{\frac{1}{6}} = 2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}} = 2^{\frac{1}{3} + \frac{2}{3}} = 2^{1} = 2$

정답: ①
