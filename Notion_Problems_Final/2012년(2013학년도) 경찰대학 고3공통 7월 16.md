## 문제
$\sum_{k=1}^{20}(2k+1) \left(\frac{1}{k}+\frac{1}{k+1}+\frac{1}{k+2}+\dots+\frac{1}{20}\right)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $250$   ② $254$   ③ $258$   ④ $262$   ⑤ $266$

## 풀이
$\sum_{k=1}^{20}(2k+1) \left(\frac{1}{k}+\frac{1}{k+1}+\frac{1}{k+2}+\dots+\frac{1}{20}\right)$

$= \sum_{k=1}^{1}(2k+1) \left(\frac{1}{1}\right) + \sum_{k=1}^{2}(2k+1) \left(\frac{1}{2}\right) + \sum_{k=1}^{3}(2k+1) \left(\frac{1}{3}\right) + \dots + \sum_{k=1}^{20}(2k+1) \left(\frac{1}{20}\right)$

$= \sum_{n=1}^{20} n(n+2) \times \frac{1}{n}$

$= \sum_{n=1}^{20}(n+2)$

$= 250$

[정답] ①
