## 문제
$\frac{1}{2 \sqrt{1} + \sqrt{2}} + \frac{1}{3 \sqrt{2} + 2 \sqrt{3}} + \cdots + \frac{1}{121 \sqrt{120} + 120 \sqrt{121}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{9}{10}$   ② $\frac{10}{11}$   ③ $\frac{11}{10}$   ④ $\frac{12}{11}$   ⑤ $\frac{6}{5}$

## 풀이
$$ \frac{1}{(n+1) \sqrt{n} + n \sqrt{n+1}} $$
$$ = \frac{(n+1) \sqrt{n} - n \sqrt{n+1}}{(n+1)^{2} n - n^{2} (n+1)} $$
$$ = \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{n+1}} $$
이므로 주어진 식의 값은
$$ \sum_{n=1}^{120} \left( \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{n+1}} \right) $$
$$ = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1}} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{121}} = 1 - \frac{1}{11} = \frac{10}{11} $$

정답: ②
