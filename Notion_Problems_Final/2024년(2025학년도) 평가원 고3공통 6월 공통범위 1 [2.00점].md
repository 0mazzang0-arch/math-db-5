## 문제
$\left( \frac{5}{\sqrt[3]{25}} \right)^{\frac{3}{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5}$   ② $\frac{\sqrt{5}}{5}$   ③ $1$   ④ $\sqrt{5}$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ \left( \frac{5}{\sqrt[3]{25}} \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} $$
$$ = \left( \frac{5}{5^{\frac{2}{3}}} \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} $$
$$ = \left( 5^{1 - \frac{2}{3}} \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} $$
$$ = \left( 5^{\frac{1}{3}} \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} $$
$$ = 5^{\frac{1}{3} \times \frac{3}{2}} $$
$$ = 5^{\frac{1}{2}} $$
$$ = \sqrt{5} $$

정답: ④
