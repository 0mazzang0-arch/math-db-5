## 문제
$3 A+B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$, $2 A-B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$를 만족하는 행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여 행렬 $A+B$의 각 성분의 합은?

## 보기
① $-1$   ② $0$   ③ $1$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $3$

## 풀이
$3 A+B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right) \cdots \cdots$ ㉠
$2 A-B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 2 & 5 \end{array} \right) \cdots \cdots$ ㉡

㉠ $+$ ㉡에서
$5 A= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 10 \end{array} \right)$ $\therefore A= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

$\therefore B = 2 A- \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$
$= 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)- \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 2 & 5 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & 1 \\ -2 & -1 \end{array} \right)$

$\therefore A+B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$

따라서 성분의 합은
$0 + 1 + (-2) + 1 = 0$
