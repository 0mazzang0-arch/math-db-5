## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{2x+1}-1}{x^{2}+x}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-1$   ② $-\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $0$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{2x+1}-1}{x^{2}+x}$
분자를 유리화하면 다음과 같다.
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{(\sqrt{2x+1}-1)(\sqrt{2x+1}+1)}{(x^{2}+x)(\sqrt{2x+1}+1)}$
$= \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{(2x+1)-1}{x(x+1)(\sqrt{2x+1}+1)}$
$= \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{2x}{x(x+1)(\sqrt{2x+1}+1)}$
$= \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{2}{(x+1)(\sqrt{2x+1}+1)}$
$= \frac{2}{(0+1)(\sqrt{2(0)+1}+1)}$
$= \frac{2}{1 \times 2} = 1$

정답은 ⑤이다.
