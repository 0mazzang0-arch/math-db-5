## 문제
$8^{\frac{5}{6}} \times 4^{-\frac{1}{4}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$$8^{\frac{5}{6}} \times 4^{-\frac{1}{4}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{5}{2}} \cdot 2^{-\frac{1}{2}} \cdot 2^{-\frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{5}{2} - \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{3}{2}}$$
$$= 2\sqrt{2}$$

정답은 ④입니다.
