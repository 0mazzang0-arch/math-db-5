## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{6x}{e^{4x} - e^{2x}}$ 의 값은? 

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
[정답] ③ 

$$ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{6x}{e^{4x} - e^{2x}} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1}{\frac{e^{4x} - 1}{6x} - \frac{e^{2x} - 1}{6x}} $$ 
$$ = \frac{1}{\frac{4}{6} - \frac{2}{6}} $$ 
$$ = \frac{1}{\frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{3}} = 3 $$
