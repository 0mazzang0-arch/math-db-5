## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sqrt{n+3} (\sqrt{n+2} - \sqrt{n+1})$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $\frac{1}{3}$   ④ $\frac{1}{4}$   ⑤ $0$

## 풀이
[정답] ②

$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sqrt{n+3} (\sqrt{n+2} - \sqrt{n+1})$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n+3} \{ (n+2) - (n+1) \}}{\sqrt{n+2} + \sqrt{n+1}}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n+3}}{\sqrt{n+2} + \sqrt{n+1}}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{1+ \frac{3}{n}}}{\sqrt{1+ \frac{2}{n}} + \sqrt{1+ \frac{1}{n}}}$
$= \frac{1}{2}$
