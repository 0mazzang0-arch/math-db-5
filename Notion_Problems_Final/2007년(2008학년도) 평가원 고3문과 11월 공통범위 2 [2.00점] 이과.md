## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$, $B = \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$ 에 대하여 $A = 2B - X$ 를 만족시키는 행렬 $X$ 는?

## 보기
① $\begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 2 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$   ② $\begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 3 & -2 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$   ③ $\begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$   ④ $\begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} -2 & -1 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$   ⑤ $\begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} -3 & 1 \\ -2 & 2 \end{array} \end{pmatrix}$

## 풀이
$A = 2B - X$ 에서 $X$ 를 구하면 다음과 같다.
$$ X = 2B - A $$
$$ = 2 \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \end{pmatrix} - \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array} \end{pmatrix} $$
$$ = \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 0 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \end{pmatrix} - \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 0 \end{array} \end{pmatrix} $$
$$ = \begin{pmatrix} \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 2 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \end{pmatrix} $$

따라서 정답은 ① 이다.
