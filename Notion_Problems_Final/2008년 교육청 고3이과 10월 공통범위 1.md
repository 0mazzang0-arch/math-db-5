## 문제
$2^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{2}{3}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$2^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times (2^2)^{\frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{4}{3}}$
$= 2^{-\frac{1}{3} + \frac{4}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{3}{3}}$
$= 2^1 = 2$

정답: ③
