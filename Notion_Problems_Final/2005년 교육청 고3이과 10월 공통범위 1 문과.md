## 문제
$2^{\frac{1}{3}} \div 3^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 18^{\frac{2}{3}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $4$   ② $6$   ③ $8$   ④ $9$   ⑤ $12$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$2^{\frac{1}{3}} \div 3^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 18^{\frac{2}{3}}$$ 
$$= 2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 3^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times (2 \times 3^{2})^{\frac{2}{3}}$$ 
$$= 2^{\frac{1}{3} + \frac{2}{3}} \times 3^{-\frac{1}{3} + \frac{4}{3}}$$
$$= 2^{1} \times 3^{1} = 6$$
