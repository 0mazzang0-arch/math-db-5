## 문제
두 행렬 $$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right), B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) $$가 있다. 행렬 $AB$로 나타내어지는 일차변환에 의하여 두 점 ($1$, $0$), ($0$, $1$)이 각각 두 점 ($0$, $2$), ($-2$, $0$)으로 옮겨질 때, $a + b + c + d$의 값은?

## 보기
① $-4$   ② $-2$   ③ $0$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$$ AB \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 2 \end{array} \right), AB \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} -2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로
$$ AB \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
즉,
$$ AB = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
이다. 따라서
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
에서
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right)^{-1} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & -2 \\ 4 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로
$\therefore a = -2$, $b = -2$, $c = 4$, $d = 2$
$\therefore a + b + c + d = 2$

정답은 ④이다.
