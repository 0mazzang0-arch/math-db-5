## 문제
세 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & \log 2 \\ \log 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & \log 5 \\ \log 5 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $C = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $(A+B)C$ 는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right)$ ② $\left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right)$ ③ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 2 \end{array} \right)$ ④ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \end{array} \right)$ ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 3 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ④ 

$$ A+B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & \log 2 \\ \log 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & \log 5 \\ \log 5 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & \log 2 + \log 5 \\ \log 2 + \log 5 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ \therefore (A+B)C = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
