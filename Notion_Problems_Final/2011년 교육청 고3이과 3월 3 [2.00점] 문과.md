## 문제
$\lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^{2} - 3x}{\sqrt{x + 1} - 2}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $0$   ② $3$   ③ $6$   ④ $9$   ⑤ $12$

## 풀이
$$ \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^{2} - 3x}{\sqrt{x + 1} - 2} $$
$$ = \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x(x - 3)(\sqrt{x + 1} + 2)}{(\sqrt{x + 1} - 2)(\sqrt{x + 1} + 2)} $$
$$ = \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x(x - 3)(\sqrt{x + 1} + 2)}{x - 3} $$
$$ = \lim_{x \to 3} x(\sqrt{x + 1} + 2) $$
$$ = 3(\sqrt{3 + 1} + 2) $$
$$ = 3 \cdot 4 = 12 $$

정답은 ⑤이다.
