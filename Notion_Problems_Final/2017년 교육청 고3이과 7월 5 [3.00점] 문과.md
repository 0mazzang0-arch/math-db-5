## 문제
$\int _{0}^{4} (5x-3) \sqrt{x} dx$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $47$   ② $48$   ③ $49$   ④ $50$   ⑤ $51$

## 풀이
$\int _{0}^{4} (5x-3) \sqrt{x} dx$
$= \int _{0}^{4} (5x^{\frac{3}{2}} - 3x^{\frac{1}{2}}) dx$
$= [ 2x^{\frac{5}{2}} - 2x^{\frac{3}{2}} ]_{0}^{4}$
$= (2 \cdot 4^{\frac{5}{2}} - 2 \cdot 4^{\frac{3}{2}}) - 0$
$= (2 \cdot 32 - 2 \cdot 8)$
$= 64 - 16$
$= 48$

정답: ②
