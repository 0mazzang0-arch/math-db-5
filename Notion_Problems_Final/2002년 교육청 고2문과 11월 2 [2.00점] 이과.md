## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{n(n+1)} - \sqrt{(n+2)(n+3)}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-1$   ② $-\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $-\frac{1}{3}$   ④ $\frac{1}{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{1}{2}$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$ \begin{array}{l} \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{n(n+1)} - \sqrt{(n+2)(n+3)}} \\ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n(n+1)} + \sqrt{(n+2)(n+3)}}{n(n+1) - (n+2)(n+3)} \\ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{n(n+1)} + \sqrt{(n+2)(n+3)}}{-4n-6} \\ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sqrt{1+\frac{1}{n}} + \sqrt{\left( 1+\frac{2}{n} \right) \left( 1+\frac{3}{n} \right)}}{-4-\frac{6}{n}} \\ = -\frac{1+1}{4} \\ = -\frac{1}{2} \end{array} $$
