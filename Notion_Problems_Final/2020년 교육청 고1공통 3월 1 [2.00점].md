## 문제
$-\frac{7}{2} \times (-3) + 4 \times \left( -\frac{5}{2} \right)$의 값은?

## 보기
① $-1$   ② $-\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $0$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
$-\frac{7}{2} \times (-3) + 4 \times \left( -\frac{5}{2} \right)$
$= \frac{7 \times 3}{2} - 4 \times \frac{5}{2}$
$= \frac{21}{2} - 10$
$= \frac{21}{2} - \frac{20}{2}$
$= \frac{1}{2}$

정답: ④
