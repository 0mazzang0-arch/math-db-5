## 문제
두 행렬 $X$, $Y$에 대하여 
$$ X + Y = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$, $$ X - Y = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 1 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \right) $$
일 때, $X^{2} + XY$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 1 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ X + Y = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \cdots ㉠ $$
$$ X - Y = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 1 \\ -1 & -2 \end{array} \right) \cdots ㉡ $$
$㉠ + ㉡$을 하면
$$ 2X = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -2 & 0 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \therefore X = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \therefore X^{2} + XY = X(X + Y) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$

정답은 ⑤입니다.
