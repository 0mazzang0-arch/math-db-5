## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 $2 A + 3 X = A + 3 B + X$ 를 만족시키는 행렬 $X$ 는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 7 & 0 \end{array} \right)$ ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -2 \\ 9 & -7 \end{array} \right)$ ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 0 & -6 \end{array} \right)$ ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$ ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 5 & -3 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$2 A + 3 X = A + 3 B + X$ 를 정리하면
$2 X = 3 B - A$
$2 X = 3 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right)$
$2 X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -6 \\ 9 & 3 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right)$
$2 X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -4 \\ 14 & 0 \end{array} \right)$
$\therefore X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 7 & 0 \end{array} \right)$

따라서 정답은 ①이다.
