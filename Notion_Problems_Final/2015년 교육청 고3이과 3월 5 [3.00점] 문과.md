## 문제
$x$, $y$에 대한 연립방정식 $\left( \begin{array}{cc} a & 0 \\ 1 & a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right)$가 $x=0$, $y=0$ 이외의 해를 가질 때, 상수 $a$의 값은?

## 보기
① $-5$   ② $-4$   ③ $-3$   ④ $-2$   ⑤ $-1$

## 풀이
$\left( \begin{array}{cc} a & 0 \\ 1 & a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right)$ 에서 

$\left( \begin{array}{cc} a & 0 \\ 1 & a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right)$ 

$\left( \begin{array}{cc} a & -1 \\ 1 & a+2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right)$ 

$x$, $y$에 대한 연립방정식이 $x=0$, $y=0$ 이외의 해를 가지므로 행렬 $\left( \begin{array}{cc} a & -1 \\ 1 & a+2 \end{array} \right)$의 역행렬이 존재하지 않는다. 

$a(a+2)-(-1) \times 1 = 0$ 

$(a+1)^{2}=0$ 

따라서 $a=-1$
