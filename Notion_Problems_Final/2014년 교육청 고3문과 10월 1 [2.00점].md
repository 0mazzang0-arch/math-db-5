## 문제
$( 3^{2} )^{\frac{1}{2}} + ( 3^{-2} )^{\frac{1}{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{8}{3}$   ② $3$   ③ $\frac{10}{3}$   ④ $\frac{11}{3}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$$ ( 3^{2} )^{\frac{1}{2}} + ( 3^{-2} )^{\frac{1}{2}} = 3^{2 \times \frac{1}{2}} + 3^{-2 \times \frac{1}{2}} = 3 + 3^{-1} = 3 + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{10}{3} $$
[정답] ③
