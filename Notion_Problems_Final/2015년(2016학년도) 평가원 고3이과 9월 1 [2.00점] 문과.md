## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & a \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 $2A + B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 4 & 7 \end{array} \right)$일 때, $a$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ 2A + B = 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & a \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 2 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 4 & a \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 4 & a + 2 \end{array} \right) $$
문제에서 $2A + B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 4 & 7 \end{array} \right)$이므로
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 4 & a + 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 4 & 7 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 $a + 2 = 7$에서 $a = 5$이다.

정답: ⑤
