## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2^{n}(2^{n+1} + 1)}{4^{n+1} + 2^{n} + 1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{4}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $1$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2^{n} ( 2^{n+1} + 1)}{ 4^{n+1} + 2^{n} + 1}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2^{2n+1} + 2^{n} }{ 2^{2n+2} + 2^{n} + 1}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2 \cdot 2^{2n} + 2^{n} }{ 4 \cdot 2^{2n} + 2^{n} + 1 }$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2 + \frac{1}{ 2^{n} } }{ 4 + \frac{1}{ 2^{n} } + \frac{1}{ 2^{2n} } }$
$= \frac{2}{4} = \frac{1}{2}$

정답: ②
