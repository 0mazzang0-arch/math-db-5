## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A$ 에 대하여 
$A^{2}-5 A+6 E=O$, 
$A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 11 \\ 1 \end{array} \right)$
이 성립할 때, 
$A \left( \begin{array}{c} -22 \\ -2 \end{array} \right) + A \left( \begin{array}{c} 20 \\ -30 \end{array} \right)$ 는? (단, $E$ 는 단위행렬이고, $O$ 는 영행렬이다.) 

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{c} -24 \\ -36 \end{array} \right)$  ② $\left( \begin{array}{c} 24 \\ 36 \end{array} \right)$  ③ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 24 \\ -36 \end{array} \right)$  ④ $\left( \begin{array}{c} -36 \\ 24 \end{array} \right)$  ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 36 \\ -24 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ③ 

$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} -22 \\ -2 \end{array} \right) + A \left( \begin{array}{c} 20 \\ -30 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = -2 A \left( \begin{array}{c} 11 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) + 10 A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -3 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = -2 A^{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -3 \end{array} \right) + 10 A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -3 \end{array} \right) = ( -2 A^{2} + 10 A ) \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -3 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = 12 E \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ -3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 24 \\ -36 \end{array} \right) $$ 

($\because -2 A^{2} + 10 A = -2 ( A^{2} - 5 A ) = -2 ( -6 E ) = 12 E$)
