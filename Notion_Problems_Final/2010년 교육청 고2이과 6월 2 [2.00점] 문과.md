## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $A+A^{-1}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 5 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & -4 \\ -4 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & 0 \\ 0 & 6 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$ 의 역행렬 $A^{-1}$ 을 구하면 다음과 같다.
$$ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{(1)(5) - (-2)(-2)} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 
$$ A+A^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & 0 \\ 0 & 6 \end{array} \right) $$
이다.

정답: ⑤
