## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $A + 2B$의 $(1, 2)$ 성분은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$ A + 2B $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) + 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 2 \\ 6 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 4 \\ 7 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$ 
따라서, 행렬 $A + 2B$의 $(1, 2)$ 성분은 $4$이다.
