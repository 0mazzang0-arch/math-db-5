## 문제
$2^{-2} \times 16^{\frac{1}{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$2^{-2} \times 16^{\frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{-2} \times (2^{4})^{\frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{-2} \times 2^{2}$$
$$= 2^{-2+2}$$
$$= 2^{0} = 1$$

[다른 풀이]
$$2^{-2} \times 16^{\frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= \frac{1}{2^{2}} \times \sqrt{16}$$
$$= \frac{1}{4} \times 4 = 1$$

정답: ①
