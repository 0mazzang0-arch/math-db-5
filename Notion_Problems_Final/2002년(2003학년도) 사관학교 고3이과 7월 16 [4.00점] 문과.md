## 문제
$P_{n}= \lim _{x \to \infty } \{ (1+\frac{1}{x} ) (1+\frac{2}{x} ) (1+\frac{3}{x} ) \cdots (1+\frac{n}{x} ) \} ^{x}$ 일 때, $\sum_{n=1}^{20} \frac{1}{\ln P_{n}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{19}{20}$   ② $\frac{21}{20}$   ③ $\frac{20}{21}$   ④ $\frac{38}{21}$   ⑤ $\frac{40}{21}$

## 풀이
$$ \begin{aligned} P_{n} &= \lim _{x \to \infty } \left( 1+\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x} \left( 1+\frac{2}{x} \right)^{x} \left( 1+\frac{3}{x} \right)^{x} \cdots \left( 1+\frac{n}{x} \right)^{x} \\ &= \lim _{x \to \infty } \left( 1+\frac{1}{x} \right)^{x} \left\{ \left( 1+\frac{2}{x} \right)^{\frac{x}{2}} \right\}^{2} \left\{ \left( 1+\frac{3}{x} \right)^{\frac{x}{3}} \right\}^{3} \cdots \left\{ \left( 1+\frac{n}{x} \right)^{\frac{x}{n}} \right\}^{n} \\ &= e^{1} \cdot e^{2} \cdot e^{3} \cdot \cdots \cdot e^{n} \\ &= e^{1+2+3+\cdots+n} \\ &= e^{\frac{n(n+1)}{2}} \end{aligned} $$

따라서 $\ln P_{n} = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$ 이다.

구하는 값은
$$ \begin{aligned} \sum_{n=1}^{20} \frac{1}{\ln P_{n}} &= \sum_{n=1}^{20} \frac{2}{n(n+1)} \\ &= 2 \sum_{n=1}^{20} \left( \frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{n+1} \right) \\ &= 2 \left( 1 - \frac{1}{21} \right) \\ &= 2 \cdot \frac{20}{21} = \frac{40}{21} \end{aligned} $$

정답은 ⑤이다.
