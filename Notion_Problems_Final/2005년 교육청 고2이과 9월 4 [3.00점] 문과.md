## 문제
등식 $$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 3 \\ 1 & x \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ y & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 4 \\ 1 & z \end{array} \right) $$ 가 성립할 때, $z$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $4$   ② $5$   ③ $6$   ④ $7$   ⑤ $8$

## 풀이
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 3 \\ 1 & x \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ y & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 4 \\ 1 & z \end{array} \right) $$ 에서 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2+3y & 4 \\ 2+xy & 1+x \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 4 \\ 1 & z \end{array} \right) $$ 

$2+3y=1$ 에서 
$y=-\frac{1}{3}$ 

$2+xy=1$ 에 $y=-\frac{1}{3}$ 을 대입하면
$2-\frac{1}{3}x=1$, 즉 $x=3$ 

따라서 $z=1+x=1+3=4$
