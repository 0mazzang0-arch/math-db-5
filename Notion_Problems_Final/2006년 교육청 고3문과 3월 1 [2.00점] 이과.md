## 문제
$\sqrt[3]{2 \sqrt{2}} \times \sqrt[6]{8}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt[3]{2}$   ③ $\sqrt{2}$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $2 \sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
$\sqrt[3]{2 \sqrt{2}} \times \sqrt[6]{8}$
$= (2 \cdot 2^{\frac{1}{2}})^{\frac{1}{3}} \times (2^{3})^{\frac{1}{6}}$
$= (2^{\frac{3}{2}})^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{3}{6}}$
$= 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 2^{\frac{1}{2}} = 2$

정답: ④
