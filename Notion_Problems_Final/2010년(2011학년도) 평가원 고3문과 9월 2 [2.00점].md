## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} (\sqrt{n^{2} + 4n + 11} - n)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} (\sqrt{n^{2} + 4n + 11} - n) $$ 
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(\sqrt{n^{2} + 4n + 11} - n)(\sqrt{n^{2} + 4n + 11} + n)}{\sqrt{n^{2} + 4n + 11} + n} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{4n + 11}{\sqrt{n^{2} + 4n + 11} + n} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{4 + \frac{11}{n}}{\sqrt{1 + \frac{4}{n} + \frac{11}{n^{2}}} + 1} $$ 
$$ = 2 $$
