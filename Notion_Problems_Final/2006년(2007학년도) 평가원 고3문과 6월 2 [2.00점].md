## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $A + 2 A^{-1}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 2 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & -2 \\ 4 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 5 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
정답: ⑤

행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 역행렬 $A^{-1}$ 을 구하면
$$ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{1 \cdot 4 - 1 \cdot 2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \frac{1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이다. 따라서 행렬 $A + 2 A^{-1}$ 은 다음과 같다.
$$ A + 2 A^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right) + 2 \cdot \frac{1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 2 & 4 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & -1 \\ -2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 0 \\ 0 & 5 \end{array} \right) $$
