## 문제
수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$ 의 일반항이 $a_{n} = \frac{1}{(3n-2)(3n+1)}$ 일 때, $\sum_{k=1}^{8} a_{k}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5}$   ② $\frac{6}{25}$   ③ $\frac{7}{25}$   ④ $\frac{8}{25}$   ⑤ $\frac{9}{25}$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$a_{n} = \frac{1}{(3n-2)(3n+1)} = \frac{1}{3} \left( \frac{1}{3n-2} - \frac{1}{3n+1} \right)$

$\sum_{k=1}^{8} a_{k} = \frac{1}{3} \sum_{k=1}^{8} \left( \frac{1}{3k-2} - \frac{1}{3k+1} \right)$

$= \frac{1}{3} \left\{ \left( \frac{1}{1} - \frac{1}{4} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{7} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{19} - \frac{1}{22} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{22} - \frac{1}{25} \right) \right\}$

$= \frac{1}{3} \left( 1 - \frac{1}{25} \right) = \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{24}{25} = \frac{8}{25}$
