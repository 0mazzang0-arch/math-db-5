## 문제
$\sin \theta + \cos \theta = \frac{2}{3}$ 일 때, $\sin^3 \theta + \cos^3 \theta$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{19}{27}$   ② $\frac{20}{27}$   ③ $\frac{7}{9}$   ④ $\frac{22}{27}$   ⑤ $\frac{23}{27}$

## 풀이
$\sin \theta + \cos \theta = \frac{2}{3}$, $\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1$ 이므로

$(\sin \theta + \cos \theta)^2 = \sin^2 \theta + 2 \sin \theta \cos \theta + \cos^2 \theta$

$\Leftrightarrow \frac{4}{9} = 1 + 2 \sin \theta \cos \theta$

$\therefore \sin \theta \cos \theta = -\frac{5}{18}$

$\sin^3 \theta + \cos^3 \theta = (\sin \theta + \cos \theta)^3 - 3 \sin \theta \cos \theta (\sin \theta + \cos \theta)$

$= \frac{8}{27} - 3 \times (-\frac{5}{18}) \times \frac{2}{3}$

$= \frac{23}{27}$

정답: ⑤
