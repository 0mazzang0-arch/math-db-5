## 문제
두 이차정사각행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -4 \\ -2 & 3 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 4 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $(A+B)^{2}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -2 & 0 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -4 \\ -2 & 3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 4 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ A+B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & -4 \\ -2 & 3 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 4 \\ 2 & -4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) = -E $$
이므로
$$ (A+B)^{2} = (-E)^{2} = E^{2} = E = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 정답은 ①이다.
