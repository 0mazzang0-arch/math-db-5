## 문제
$(-\sqrt{2})^{4} \times 8^{-\frac{2}{3}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ (-\sqrt{2})^{4} \times 8^{-\frac{2}{3}} $$
$$ =(-1)^{4} \times (2^{\frac{1}{2}})^{4} \times (2^{3})^{-\frac{2}{3}} $$
$$ =1 \times 2^{\frac{1}{2} \times 4} \times 2^{3 \times (-\frac{2}{3})} $$
$$ =2^{2} \times 2^{-2} $$
$$ =2^{2+(-2)} $$
$$ =2^{0} $$
$$ =1 $$

정답: ①
