## 문제
$\sqrt{3+\sqrt{8}}+\sqrt{3-\sqrt{8}}$ 을 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $2+\sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
$\sqrt{3+\sqrt{8}} = \sqrt{3+2\sqrt{2}}$
$= \sqrt{(\sqrt{2}+1)^{2}} = \sqrt{2}+1$

$\sqrt{3-\sqrt{8}} = \sqrt{3-2\sqrt{2}}$
$= \sqrt{(\sqrt{2}-1)^{2}} = \sqrt{2}-1$

따라서
$\sqrt{3+\sqrt{8}}+\sqrt{3-\sqrt{8}} = \sqrt{2}+1+\sqrt{2}-1$
$= 2\sqrt{2}$

정답: ④
