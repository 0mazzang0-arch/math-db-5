## 문제
$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}} \tan x \cos x dx$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{3}{4}$   ② $\frac{4-\sqrt{2}}{4}$   ③ $\frac{4-\sqrt{3}}{4}$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $\frac{4-\sqrt{5}}{4}$

## 풀이
$\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}} \tan x \cos x dx$
$= \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}} \frac{\sin x}{\cos x} \cdot \cos x dx$
$= \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}} \sin x dx$
$= [-\cos x]_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}$
$= (-\cos \frac{\pi}{3}) - (-\cos 0)$
$= (-\frac{1}{2}) - (-1)$
$= \frac{1}{2}$

정답: $④$
