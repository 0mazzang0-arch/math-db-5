## 문제

다음 식을 간단히 하면?

$$10^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 2^{-\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{6}}$$

## 보기

① $\sqrt{2}$   ② $2$   ③ $\sqrt{5}$   ④ $5$   ⑤ $\sqrt{10}$

## 풀이

$$10^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 2^{-\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{6}}$$
$$= (2 \times 5)^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 2^{-\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{6}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 2^{-\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{6}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{2}{3} - \frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{\frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{6}}$$
$$= 2^{0} \times 5^{\frac{1}{2}}$$
$$= \sqrt{5}$$

정답: ③
