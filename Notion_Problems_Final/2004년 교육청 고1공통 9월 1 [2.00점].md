## 문제
$\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}+1}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}-1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}+1}+\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}-1}$
$= \frac{\sqrt{2}-1}{(\sqrt{2}+1)(\sqrt{2}-1)}+\frac{\sqrt{2}+1}{(\sqrt{2}-1)(\sqrt{2}+1)}$
$= \sqrt{2}-1+\sqrt{2}+1$
$= 2\sqrt{2}$

[정답] ④
