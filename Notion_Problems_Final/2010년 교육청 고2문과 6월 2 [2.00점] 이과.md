## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $A + A^{-1}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 5 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & -4 \\ -4 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 6 & 0 \\ 0 & 6 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right)$ 의 역행렬 $A^{-1}$ 을 구하면
$$ \begin{array}{ll} A^{-1} & = \dfrac{1}{1 \times 5 - (-2) \times (-2)} \left( \begin{array}{rr} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) \\ & = \dfrac{1}{5 - 4} \left( \begin{array}{rr} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) \\ & = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
따라서 행렬 $A + A^{-1}$ 은
$$ \begin{array}{ll} A + A^{-1} & = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & -2 \\ -2 & 5 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 5 & 2 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) \\ & = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1+5 & -2+2 \\ -2+2 & 5+1 \end{array} \right) \\ & = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 6 & 0 \\ 0 & 6 \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
정답은 ⑤이다.
