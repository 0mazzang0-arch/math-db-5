## 문제
$8^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 27^{-\frac{1}{3}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{7}{6}$   ② $\frac{4}{3}$   ③ $\frac{3}{2}$   ④ $\frac{5}{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{11}{6}$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$
\begin{array}{l}
8^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 27^{-\frac{1}{3}} \\
= (2^{3})^{\frac{2}{3}} \times (3^{3})^{-\frac{1}{3}} \\
= 2^{2} \times 3^{-1} \\
= 4 \times \frac{1}{3} \\
= \frac{4}{3}
\end{array}
$$
