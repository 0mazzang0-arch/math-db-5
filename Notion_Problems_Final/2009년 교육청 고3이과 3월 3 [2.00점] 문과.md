## 문제
$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1+2^{n}}{3^{n}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$  ② $\frac{3}{2}$  ③ $2$  ④ $\frac{5}{2}$  ⑤ $3$

## 풀이
$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1+2^{n}}{3^{n}} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left( \frac{1}{3} \right)^{n} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left( \frac{2}{3} \right)^{n}$$
$$= \frac{\frac{1}{3}}{1 - \frac{1}{3}} + \frac{\frac{2}{3}}{1 - \frac{2}{3}}$$
$$= \frac{\frac{1}{3}}{\frac{2}{3}} + \frac{\frac{2}{3}}{\frac{1}{3}}$$
$$= \frac{1}{2} + 2 = \frac{5}{2}$$

정답: ④
