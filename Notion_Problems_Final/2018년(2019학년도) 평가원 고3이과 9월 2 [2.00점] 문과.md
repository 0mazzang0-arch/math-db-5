## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x} - 1}{x ( x^{2} + 2 )}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $\frac{1}{3}$   ④ $\frac{1}{4}$   ⑤ $\frac{1}{5}$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x} - 1}{x ( x^{2} + 2 )} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{x \to 0} \left( \frac{e^{x}-1}{x} \times \frac{1}{x^{2}+2} \right) $$ 
$$ = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x}-1}{x} \times \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1}{x^{2}+2} $$ 
$$ = 1 \times \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{2} $$
