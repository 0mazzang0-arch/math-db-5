## 문제
정적분 $\int_{0}^{1} \left( \frac{x^{4}}{x^{2}+1} - \frac{1}{x^{2}+1} \right) dx$의 값은?

## 보기
① $-\frac{5}{6}$   ② $-\frac{2}{3}$   ③ $\frac{1}{2}$   ④ $\frac{2}{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{5}{6}$

## 풀이
$$ \int_{0}^{1} \left( \frac{x^{4}}{x^{2} + 1} - \frac{1}{x^{2} + 1} \right) dx = \int_{0}^{1} \frac{x^{4} - 1}{x^{2} + 1} dx $$
$$ = \int_{0}^{1} \frac{(x^{2} + 1)(x^{2} - 1)}{x^{2} + 1} dx = \int_{0}^{1} (x^{2} - 1) dx $$
$$ = \left[ \frac{1}{3} x^{3} - x \right]_{0}^{1} = -\frac{2}{3} $$
정답: ②
