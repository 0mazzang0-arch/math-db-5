## 문제
$\left( \frac{4}{2^{\sqrt{2}}} \right)^{2+\sqrt{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{4}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $1$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$\left( \frac{4}{2^{\sqrt{2}}} \right)^{2+\sqrt{2}}$
$= \left( \frac{2^{2}}{2^{\sqrt{2}}} \right)^{2+\sqrt{2}}$
$= \left( 2^{2-\sqrt{2}} \right)^{2+\sqrt{2}}$
$= 2^{(2-\sqrt{2})(2+\sqrt{2})}$
$= 2^{2^{2}-(\sqrt{2})^{2}}$
$= 2^{4-2}$
$= 2^{2}$
$= 4$

정답: ⑤
