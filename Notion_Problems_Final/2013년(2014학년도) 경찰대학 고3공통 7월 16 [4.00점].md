## 문제
첫째항이 $3$인 등차수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$에 대하여 $a_{10} - a_{2} = 4$일 때, $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{a_{n} a_{n+1} a_{n+2}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{21}$   ② $\frac{2}{21}$   ③ $\frac{1}{7}$   ④ $\frac{4}{21}$   ⑤ $\frac{5}{21}$

## 풀이
등차수열 $\{ a_{n} \}$의 공차를 $d$라 하면
$a_{10} - a_{2} = 8d = 4$
$\therefore d = \frac{1}{2}$
$\therefore a_{n} = \frac{n+5}{2}$ ($\because a_{1} = 3$)

$\therefore \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{a_{n} a_{n+1} a_{n+2}}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{a_{k} a_{k+1} a_{k+2}}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{8}{(k+5)(k+6)(k+7)}$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} 4 \left( \frac{1}{(k+5)(k+6)} - \frac{1}{(k+6)(k+7)} \right)$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} 4 \left( \frac{1}{42} - \frac{1}{(n+6)(n+7)} \right)$
$= \frac{2}{21}$

[정답] ②
