## 문제
$\sum_{k=1}^{10} \frac{1}{k^{2}-k+1} - \sum_{k=1}^{10} \frac{1}{k^{2}+k+1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{83}{90}$   ② $\frac{90}{101}$   ③ $\frac{91}{101}$   ④ $\frac{101}{111}$   ⑤ $\frac{110}{111}$

## 풀이
[정답] ⑤

$$ \sum_{k=1}^{10} \frac{1}{k^{2}-k+1} - \sum_{k=1}^{10} \frac{1}{k^{2}+k+1} $$

$$ = \left( 1 + \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{7} + \cdots + \frac{1}{91} \right) - \left( \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{7} + \cdots + \frac{1}{91} + \frac{1}{111} \right) $$

$$ = 1 - \frac{1}{111} = \frac{110}{111} $$
