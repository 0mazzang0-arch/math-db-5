## 문제
실수 $a$, $b$에 대하여 두 행렬 $A$, $B$를 
$$ \begin{array}{cc} A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ -b & a \end{array} \right), & B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} b & -a \\ a & b \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
라 하자. $A+B=E$일 때, $(AB)^{-1}$의 모든 성분의 합은? (단, $E$는 단위행렬이다.)

## 보기
① $-4$   ② $-2$   ③ $0$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$A+B=E$ 이면 
$$ A+B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ -b & a \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} b & -a \\ a & b \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a+b & b-a \\ -b+a & a+b \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 
$a+b=1$, $b-a=0$
$\therefore a=b=\frac{1}{2}$

$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ -b & a \end{array} \right) = \frac{1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$
$B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} b & -a \\ a & b \end{array} \right) = \frac{1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$
이므로 
$$ AB = \frac{1}{4} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \frac{1}{4} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) = \frac{1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \frac{1}{2} E $$
$\therefore (AB)^{-1} = \left( \frac{1}{2} E \right)^{-1} = 2 E = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) $
따라서 $(AB)^{-1}$의 모든 성분의 합은 $2+0+0+2=4$이다.

정답: ⑤
