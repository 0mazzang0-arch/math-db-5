## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} \frac{k^{2} (5 k^{2}+3 )}{n^{3} (n^{2}+1 )}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $31$   ② $32$   ③ $33$   ④ $34$   ⑤ $35$

## 풀이
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} \frac{k^{2} (5 k^{2}+3 )}{n^{3} (n^{2}+1 )}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} \frac{5 k^{4}+3 k^{2}}{n^{3} (n^{2}+1 )}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \left\{ \frac{n^{2}}{n^{2}+1} \cdot \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} 5 \left(\frac{k}{n}\right)^{4} \cdot \frac{1}{n} + \frac{1}{n^{2}+1} \cdot \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} 3 \left(\frac{k}{n}\right)^{2} \cdot \frac{1}{n} \right\}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{n^{2}}{n^{2}+1} \cdot \lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} 5 \left(\frac{k}{n}\right)^{4} \cdot \frac{1}{n} + \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n^{2}+1} \cdot \lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{2 n} 3 \left(\frac{k}{n}\right)^{2} \cdot \frac{1}{n}$
$= 1 \cdot \int_{0}^{2} 5 x^{4} dx + 0 \cdot \int_{0}^{2} 3 x^{2} dx$
$= [ x^{5} ]_{0}^{2} = 2^{5} = 32$

따라서 정답은 ②이다.
