## 문제
역행렬이 존재하는 이차정사각행렬 $A$ 가 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & a \end{array} \right) A^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$, $$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} b \\ 3 \end{array} \right) $$ 
을 만족시킬 때, 두 상수 $a$, $b$ 의 곱 $ab$ 의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & a \end{array} \right) A^{-1} = -E $$ 
양변에 행렬 $A$ 를 곱하면 
$$ \therefore A = - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 1 & a \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ -1 & -a \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} b \\ 3 \end{array} \right) $$ 에서 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ -1 & -a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} b \\ 3 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$\therefore a = -3$, $b = -4$ 
$\therefore ab = 12$

정답: $12$
