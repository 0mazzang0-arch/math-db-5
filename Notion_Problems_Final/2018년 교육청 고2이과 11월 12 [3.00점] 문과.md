## 문제
함수 $f(x) = 3x^2 - 4x + 6$ 에 대하여 $\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} f \left( 1 + \frac{2k}{n} \right)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $9$   ② $10$   ③ $11$   ④ $12$   ⑤ $13$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} f \left( 1 + \frac{2k}{n} \right)$
$= \frac{1}{2} \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} f \left( 1 + \frac{2k}{n} \right)$
$= \frac{1}{2} \int_{1}^{3} f(x) dx$
$= \frac{1}{2} [x^{3} - 2x^{2} + 6x]_{1}^{3}$
$= \frac{1}{2} \{ (27 - 18 + 18) - (1 - 2 + 6) \}$
$= \frac{1}{2} (27 - 5)$
$= 11$

정답: ③
