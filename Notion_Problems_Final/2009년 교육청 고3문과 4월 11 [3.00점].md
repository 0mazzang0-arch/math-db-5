## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{1^{2} + 3^{2} + 5^{2} + \dots + (2n-1)^{2}}{2^{2} + 4^{2} + 6^{2} + \dots + (2n)^{2} + \dots + (4n)^{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{8}$   ② $\frac{1}{4}$   ③ $\frac{1}{2}$   ④ $1$   ⑤ $2$

## 풀이
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sum _{k=1}^{n} (2k-1)^{2}}{\sum _{k=1}^{2n} (2k)^{2}} = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\sum _{k=1}^{n} (4k^{2} - 4k + 1)}{\sum _{k=1}^{2n} 4k^{2}}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\frac{4n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6} - \frac{4n(n+1)}{2} + n}{\frac{4 \cdot 2n(2n+1)(4n+1)}{6}}$
$= \frac{1}{8}$

정답: ①
