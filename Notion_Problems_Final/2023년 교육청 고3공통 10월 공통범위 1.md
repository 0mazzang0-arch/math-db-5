## 문제
$2^{\sqrt{2}} \times \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{\sqrt{2}-1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$2^{\sqrt{2}} \times \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{\sqrt{2}-1} = 2^{\sqrt{2}} \times 2^{-(\sqrt{2}-1)} = 2^{\sqrt{2}} \times 2^{-\sqrt{2}+1} = 2^{\sqrt{2} - \sqrt{2} + 1} = 2^{1} = 2$

정답: ③
