## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -2 & 4 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 3 \\ 2 & 2 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $A - 2B$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & -7 \\ 6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & -7 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & 7 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & 7 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & -7 \\ 6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
두 행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여 $A - 2B$를 계산하면 다음과 같다.
$$ A - 2B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -2 & 4 \end{array} \right) - 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 3 \\ 2 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ -2 & 4 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} -4 & 6 \\ 4 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 - (-4) & -1 - 6 \\ -2 - 4 & 4 - 4 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & -7 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 정답은 ②이다.
