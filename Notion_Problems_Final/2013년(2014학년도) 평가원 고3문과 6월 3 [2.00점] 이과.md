## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{5 \times 7^{n+1} + 3}{7^n}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $15$   ② $20$   ③ $25$   ④ $30$   ⑤ $35$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{5 \times 7^{n+1} + 3}{7^n} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \left( \frac{5 \times 7 \times 7^n}{7^n} + \frac{3}{7^n} \right)$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \left( 35 + \frac{3}{7^n} \right)$
$= 35 + 0 = 35$

정답: ⑤
