## 문제
$\log _{2} 4 \times \log _{4} 2^{-2}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-4$   ② $-2$   ③ $\frac{1}{4}$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
[정답] ②

$$ \log _{2} 4 \times \log _{4} 2^{-2} $$
$$ = \log _{2} 2^{2} \times \log _{4} 4^{-1} $$
$$ = 2 \times (-1) = -2 $$

[다른 풀이]
$$ \log _{2} 4 \times \log _{4} 2^{-2} $$
$$ = \log _{2} 4 \times (-2 \log _{4} 2) $$
$$ = -2 \times \log _{2} 4 \times \log _{4} 2 $$
$$ = -2 \left( \log _{2} 4 \times \frac{1}{\log _{2} 4} \right) $$
$$ = -2 $$
