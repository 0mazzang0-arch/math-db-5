## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} (\sqrt{n^2 + 2n + 3} - n)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $\frac{3}{2}$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $2\sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
[정답] ① 

$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} (\sqrt{n^2 + 2n + 3} - n) $$ 
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(\sqrt{n^2 + 2n + 3} - n)(\sqrt{n^2 + 2n + 3} + n)}{\sqrt{n^2 + 2n + 3} + n} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{2n + 3}{\sqrt{n^2 + 2n + 3} + n} = 1 $$
