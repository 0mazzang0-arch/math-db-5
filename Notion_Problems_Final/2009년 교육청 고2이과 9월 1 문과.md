## 문제
$\sqrt[4]{4^{3}} \times \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{-\frac{3}{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $2$   ② $4$   ③ $8$   ④ $16$   ⑤ $32$

## 풀이
[정답] ③

$\sqrt[4]{4^{3}} \times \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^{-\frac{3}{2}} = 4^{\frac{3}{4}} \times 2^{\frac{3}{2}} = (2^{2})^{\frac{3}{4}} \times 2^{\frac{3}{2}} = 2^{\frac{3}{2}} \times 2^{\frac{3}{2}} = 2^{\frac{3}{2} + \frac{3}{2}} = 2^{3} = 8$
