## 문제
무한급수 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n}{n^{4}+n^{2}+1}$ 의 값을 구하면?

## 풀이
주어진 식을 변형하면 

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{n}{n^{4}+n^{2}+1}$$ 
$$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{1}{k^{2}-k+1} - \frac{1}{k^{2}+k+1} \right)$$ 

$$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{2} \left\{ \left( 1 - \frac{1}{3} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{7} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{n^{2}-n+1} - \frac{1}{n^{2}+n+1} \right) \right\}$$ 

$$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{2} \left( 1 - \frac{1}{n^{2}+n+1} \right) = \frac{1}{2}$$

정답: $\frac{1}{2}$
