## 문제
$\sum _{n=1} ^{\infty } \frac{2}{n(n+2)}$ 의 값은? 

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\frac{3}{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $\frac{5}{2}$   ⑤ $3$

## 풀이
$\sum _{n=1} ^{\infty } \frac{2}{n(n+2)}$
$= \sum _{n=1} ^{\infty } \left( \frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{n+2} \right)$ 
$= \left( \frac{1}{1} - \frac{1}{3} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{4} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{5} \right) + \cdots$ 
$= \lim _{n \to \infty } \left( 1 + \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{n+1} - \frac{1}{n+2} \right)$ 
$= \frac{3}{2}$

정답: ②
