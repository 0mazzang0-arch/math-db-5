## 문제
함수 $f(x) = \frac{x^{3}}{9}$의 역함수를 $g(x)$라 할 때, $\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} g \left( \frac{3k}{n} \right) \frac{1}{n}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{9}{4}$   ② $\frac{15}{4}$   ③ $\frac{21}{4}$   ④ $\frac{27}{4}$   ⑤ $\frac{33}{4}$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} g \left( \frac{3k}{n} \right) \frac{1}{n} = \frac{1}{3} \int_{0}^{3} g(x) dx$

한편,
$\int_{0}^{3} g(x) dx = 9 - \int_{0}^{3} f(x) dx$
($\because \frac{x^{3}}{9} = x, x = 3$)

$\frac{1}{3} \int_{0}^{3} g(x) dx$
$= \frac{1}{3} \left( 9 - \int_{0}^{3} f(x) dx \right)$
$= \frac{1}{3} \left( 9 - \left[ \frac{x^{4}}{36} \right]_{0}^{3} \right) = \frac{9}{4}$

정답: ①
