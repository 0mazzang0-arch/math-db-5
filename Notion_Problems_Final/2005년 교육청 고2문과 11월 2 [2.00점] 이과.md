## 문제
$( \log _{2} 10 ) ( \log _{5} 10 ) - ( \log _{4} 25 + \log _{25} 4 )$ 를 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $0$   ② $2$   ③ $4$   ④ $2 \log _{5} 2$   ⑤ $2 \log _{2} 5$

## 풀이
$( \log _{2} 10 ) ( \log _{5} 10 ) - ( \log _{4} 25 + \log _{25} 4 )$
$= ( 1 + \log _{2} 5 ) ( 1 + \log _{5} 2 ) - ( \log _{2^{2}} 5^{2} + \log _{5^{2}} 2^{2} )$
$= 1 + \log _{2} 5 + \log _{5} 2 + \log _{2} 5 \cdot \log _{5} 2 - \log _{2} 5 - \log _{5} 2$
$= 1 + \log _{2} 5 \cdot \frac{1}{\log _{2} 5}$
$= 1 + 1 = 2$

정답은 ②
