## 문제
함수 $f(x)$ 가
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{f(x)}{\ln(1+x)} = 1$$
을 만족시킬 때, <보기>에서 항상 옳은 것만을 있는 대로 고른 것은?

ㄱ. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{f(x)} = 0$
ㄴ. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{f(x)+x}{\ln(1+x)} = 2$
ㄷ. $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\{f(x)\}^2}{\ln(1+x)} = 0$

## 보기
① $ㄱ$   ② $ㄴ$   ③ $ㄱ, ㄴ$   ④ $ㄴ, ㄷ$   ⑤ $ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄷ$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

ㄱ. 
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{f(x)}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{\ln(1+x) \cdot \frac{f(x)}{\ln(1+x)}}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left( \frac{\sin x}{\ln(1+x)} \cdot \frac{1}{\frac{f(x)}{\ln(1+x)}} \right)$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left( \frac{\frac{\sin x}{x}}{\frac{\ln(1+x)}{x}} \cdot \frac{1}{\frac{f(x)}{\ln(1+x)}} \right)$$
$$= \frac{1}{1} \cdot \frac{1}{1} = 1$$
$\therefore$ 거짓

ㄴ. 
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{f(x)+x}{\ln(1+x)}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{f(x)}{\ln(1+x)} + \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x}{\ln(1+x)}$$
$$= 1 + \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1}{\frac{\ln(1+x)}{x}} = 1 + 1 = 2$$
$\therefore$ 참

ㄷ. 
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\{f(x)\}^2}{\ln(1+x)} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\{f(x)\}^2 \cdot \ln(1+x)}{\{\ln(1+x)\}^2}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left\{ \frac{f(x)}{\ln(1+x)} \right\}^2 \cdot \ln(1+x)$$
$$= 1^2 \cdot \lim_{x \to 0} \ln(1+x) = 1 \cdot 0 = 0$$
$\therefore$ 참

따라서 옳은 것은 ㄴ, ㄷ이다.
