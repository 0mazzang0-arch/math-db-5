## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 등식 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이 성립할 때, $x$, $y$에 대한 연립일차방정식 
$$ \left\{ \begin{array}{l} ax + by = -2 \\ cx + dy = -1 \end{array} \right. $$
의 해를 $x = \alpha$, $y = \beta$라 하자. 이때, $\alpha + \beta$의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기

## 풀이
[정답] 
$$ -14 $$

[해설] 
$$ \left\{ \begin{array}{l} ax + by = -2 \\ cx + dy = -1 \end{array} \right. $$ 
을 행렬로 나타내면 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -1 \end{array} \right) \quad \therefore \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = A^{-1} \left( \begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -1 \end{array} \right) \cdots ㉠ $$ 
한편, 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 
$$ A^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) \cdots ㉡ $$ 
$㉠$, $㉡$에서 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} -2 \\ -1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} -4 \\ -10 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ \therefore \alpha + \beta = -14 $$
