## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$와 두 수열 $\{ x_{n} \}$, $\{ y_{n} \}$에 대하여
$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x_{n} \\ y_{n} \end{array} \right) = A^{n} \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이 성립할 때, $\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(x_{n})^{2}}{y_{n}+1}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $6$   ② $7$   ③ $8$   ④ $9$   ⑤ $10$

## 풀이
$$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A^{2} = AA = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{2} & 0 \\ 0 & 4^{2} \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A^{3} = A^{2} A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{2} & 0 \\ 0 & 4^{2} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{3} & 0 \\ 0 & 4^{3} \end{array} \right) $$
$\cdots$
$$ A^{n} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{n} & 0 \\ 0 & 4^{n} \end{array} \right) (n \geq 1) $$
그러므로
$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x_{n} \\ y_{n} \end{array} \right) = A^{n} \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{n} & 0 \\ 0 & 4^{n} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \cdot 2^{n} \\ 4^{n} \end{array} \right) $$
$\therefore x_{n} = 3 \cdot 2^{n}$, $y_{n} = 4^{n}$
따라서,
$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{(x_{n})^{2}}{y_{n}+1} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3^{2} \cdot 4^{n}}{4^{n}+1} $$
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3^{2}}{1 + \left( \frac{1}{4} \right)^{n}} $$
$$ = 9 $$

정답: ④
