## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 $A^{n} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a_{n} & 0 \\ 0 & b_{n} \end{array} \right)$ 이라 할 때, $\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3 a_{n} - 2 b_{n}}{2 a_{n} + 3 b_{n}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-\frac{3}{2}$   ② $-\frac{2}{3}$   ③ $0$   ④ $\frac{2}{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{3}{2}$

## 풀이
$$ \begin{array}{l} A^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{2} & 0 \\ 0 & 3^{2} \end{array} \right) \\ A^{3} = A^{2} A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{2} & 0 \\ 0 & 3^{2} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{3} & 0 \\ 0 & 3^{3} \end{array} \right) \\ \cdots \\ A^{n} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2^{n} & 0 \\ 0 & 3^{n} \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
이므로 $a_{n} = 2^{n}$, $b_{n} = 3^{n}$ 이다.
$$ \therefore \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3 a_{n} - 2 b_{n}}{2 a_{n} + 3 b_{n}} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3 \cdot 2^{n} - 2 \cdot 3^{n}}{2 \cdot 2^{n} + 3 \cdot 3^{n}} $$
분모, 분자를 $3^{n}$ 으로 나누면
$$ = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3 \cdot \left( \frac{2}{3} \right)^{n} - 2}{2 \cdot \left( \frac{2}{3} \right)^{n} + 3} = \frac{3 \cdot 0 - 2}{2 \cdot 0 + 3} = -\frac{2}{3} $$
