## 문제
$\log _{2} \frac{8}{5} + \log _{4} \frac{25}{4}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\frac{3}{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $\frac{5}{2}$   ⑤ $3$

## 풀이
(주어진 식)
$$= \log_{2} \frac{8}{5} + \log_{4} \frac{25}{4}$$
$$= \log_{2} \frac{8}{5} + \log_{2^{2}} \left(\frac{5}{2}\right)^{2}$$
$$= \log_{2} \frac{8}{5} + \log_{2} \frac{5}{2}$$
$$= \log_{2} \left(\frac{8}{5} \times \frac{5}{2}\right)$$
$$= \log_{2} \frac{8}{2}$$
$$= \log_{2} 4$$
$$= 2$$

정답: ③
