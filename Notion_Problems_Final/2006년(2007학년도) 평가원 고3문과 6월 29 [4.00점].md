## 문제
두 이차정사각행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여 $AB-BA= \left( \begin{array}{cc} p & q \\ r & s \end{array} \right)$라 할 때, <보기>에서 항상 옳은 것을 모두 고른 것은?

<보기>
ㄱ. $A= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right)$이면 $ps-qr=0$이다.
ㄴ. 모든 이차정사각행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여 $p+s=0$이다.
ㄷ. 행렬 $AB-BA$가 영행렬이면 $B$는 $A$의 역행렬이다.

## 보기
① $ㄱ$   ② $ㄴ$   ③ $ㄱ, ㄴ$   ④ $ㄴ, ㄷ$   ⑤ $ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄷ$

## 풀이
[정답] ②

㉠ 〈반례〉
$A= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right)$라 하면
$$ AB-BA $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 6 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 3 & 3 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 5 \\ -3 & -3 \end{array} \right) $$
$\therefore ps-qr = -9 - (-15) = 6 \neq 0$ (거짓)

㉡ $A= \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$, $B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} s & t \\ w & z \end{array} \right)$라 하면
$$ AB-BA $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} as+bw & at+bz \\ cs+dw & ct+dz \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} as+ct & bs+dt \\ aw+cz & bw+dz \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} bw-ct & (a-d)t+(z-s)b \\ (d-a)w+(s-z)c & ct-bw \end{array} \right) $$
$\therefore p+s = bw-ct+ct-bw = 0$ (참)

㉢ 〈반례〉
$A= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right)$, $B= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$라 하면
$$ AB-BA $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
하지만 $A^{-1} = \frac{1}{4-6} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & -2 \\ -3 & 1 \end{array} \right) \neq B$ (거짓)
