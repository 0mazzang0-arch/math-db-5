## 문제
$\sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{2} - 12 \right)^{2} } - \sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{2} + 10 \right)^{2} }$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$\frac{1}{2} - 12 < 0$ 이므로 
$\sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{2} - 12 \right)^{2} } = - \left( \frac{1}{2} - 12 \right)$

$\frac{1}{2} + 10 > 0$ 이므로 
$\sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{2} + 10 \right)^{2} } = \frac{1}{2} + 10$

따라서 
$\sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{2} - 12 \right)^{2} } - \sqrt{ \left( \frac{1}{2} + 10 \right)^{2} }$
$= - \left( \frac{1}{2} - 12 \right) - \left( \frac{1}{2} + 10 \right)$
$= - \frac{1}{2} + 12 - \frac{1}{2} - 10$
$= 1$

정답: ①
