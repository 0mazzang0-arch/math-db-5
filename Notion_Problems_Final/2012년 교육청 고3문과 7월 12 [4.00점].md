## 문제
함수 $f(x) = (x-1)(x-2)(x-3) \cdots (x-10)$ 에 대하여 $\frac{f'(1)}{f'(4)}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-80$   ② $-84$   ③ $-88$   ④ $-92$   ⑤ $-96$

## 풀이
$$ \begin{array}{ll} f'(x) & = (x-2)(x-3) \cdots (x-10) \\ & + (x-1)(x-3) \cdots (x-10) \\ & \vdots \\ & + (x-1)(x-2) \cdots (x-9) \end{array} $$
$f'(1) = (1-2)(1-3) \cdots (1-10)$
$f'(4) = (4-1)(4-2)(4-3)(4-5) \cdots (4-10)$
$\frac{f'(1)}{f'(4)} = \frac{(-7) \times (-8) \times (-9)}{3 \times 2 \times 1} = -84$

[다른 풀이]
$f'(1) = \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{f(x)-f(1)}{x-1}$
$= \lim_{x \to 1} (x-2)(x-3) \cdots (x-10)$
$= -9!$

$f'(4) = \lim_{x \to 4} \frac{f(x)-f(4)}{x-4}$
$= \lim_{x \to 4} (x-1)(x-2)(x-3)(x-5) \cdots (x-10)$
$= 3! \times 6!$

따라서 $\frac{f'(1)}{f'(4)} = \frac{-9!}{3! \times 6!} = -84$

정답: ②
