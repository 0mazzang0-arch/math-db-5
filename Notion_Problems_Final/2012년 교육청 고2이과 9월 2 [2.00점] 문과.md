## 문제
두 행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여
$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $B-A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$일 때, $BA-A^{2}$은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 4 \\ 2 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 4 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 4 \\ 4 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 5 \\ 4 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$BA-A^{2} = (B-A)A$이므로 주어진 행렬을 대입하면 다음과 같다.
$$ BA-A^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 \times 1 + 1 \times 2 & 3 \times 1 + 1 \times 1 \\ 0 \times 1 + 1 \times 2 & 0 \times 1 + 1 \times 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 4 \\ 2 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 정답은 ③이다.
