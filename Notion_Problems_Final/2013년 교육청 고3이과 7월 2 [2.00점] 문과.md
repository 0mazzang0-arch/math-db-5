## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\sqrt{3x+1} - \sqrt{x+3}}{x^{2} - 1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-\frac{3}{4}$  ② $-\frac{1}{4}$  ③ $0$  ④ $\frac{1}{4}$  ⑤ $\frac{3}{4}$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$\lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\sqrt{3x+1} - \sqrt{x+3}}{x^{2} - 1}$$
$$= \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{2x-2}{(x-1)(x+1)(\sqrt{3x+1} + \sqrt{x+3})}$$
$$= \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{2}{(x+1)(\sqrt{3x+1} + \sqrt{x+3})} = \frac{1}{4}$$
