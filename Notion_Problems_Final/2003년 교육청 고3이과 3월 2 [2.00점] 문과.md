## 문제
$8^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 4^{\frac{1}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{6}}$ 을 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $\sqrt{2}$   ② $2$   ③ $2\sqrt{2}$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $4\sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
정답: ④

$8^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 4^{\frac{1}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{6}} = (2^{3})^{\frac{1}{2}} \times (2^{2})^{\frac{1}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{6}}$
$= 2^{\frac{3}{2}} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{6}}$
$= 2^{\frac{3}{2} + \frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{6}}$
$= 2^{\frac{9+4-1}{6}}$
$= 2^{\frac{12}{6}}$
$= 2^{2} = 4$
