## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \left( \sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} - n \right)$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{8}$   ② $\frac{1}{4}$   ③ $\frac{\sqrt{2}}{4}$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}$

## 풀이
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \left( \sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} - n \right)$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\left( \sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} - n \right) \left( \sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} + n \right)}{\sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} + n}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\left( n^{2} + \frac{n}{2} \right) - n^{2}}{\sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} + n}$
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\frac{n}{2}}{\sqrt{n^{2} + \frac{n}{2}} + n}$
분모, 분자를 $n$으로 나누면
$= \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{\frac{1}{2}}{\sqrt{1 + \frac{1}{2n}} + 1}$
$= \frac{\frac{1}{2}}{1 + 1} = \frac{1}{4}$

[정답] ②
