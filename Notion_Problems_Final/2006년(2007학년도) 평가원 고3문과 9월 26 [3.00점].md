## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A$ 가
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) , A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
를 만족시킬 때, 연립일차방정식
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 7 \\ 5 \end{array} \right) $$
의 해는 $x=p$, $y=q$ 이다. $p+q$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) , A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) + A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 7 \\ 5 \end{array} \right) $$
행렬의 선형성에 의하여
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1+1 \\ 2+1 \end{array} \right) = A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 7 \\ 5 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 $x=2$, $y=3$ 이므로 $p=2$, $q=3$ 이다.
그러므로 $p+q=2+3=5$
