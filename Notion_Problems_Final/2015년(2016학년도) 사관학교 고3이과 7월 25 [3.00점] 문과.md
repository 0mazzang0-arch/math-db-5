## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A$가 다음 조건을 만족시킨다. (단, $E$는 단위행렬이다.)

$$ \begin{array}{|l|} \hline \text{(가) } A-E \text{의 역행렬은 } A-3E \text{이다.} \\ \text{(나) } A \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) \\ \hline \end{array} $$

$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) $$ 를 만족시키는 실수 $x$, $y$에 대하여 $x+y$의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
조건 (가)에 의하여 
$$ (A-E)(A-3E) = E $$
$$ A^{2} - 4A + 3E = E $$
$$ A^{2} = 4A - 2E \cdots \cdots ㉠ $$

조건 (나)에서 $$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$ 이므로
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = A^{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = (4A - 2E) \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = 4A \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) - 2E \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = 4 \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) - 2 \left( \begin{array}{c} -1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{c} 10 \\ -4 \end{array} \right) $$

$\therefore A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -2 \end{array} \right) $

$\therefore A \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = 3A \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = 3 \left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ -2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 15 \\ -6 \end{array} \right) $

$\therefore x = 15, y = -6$
$\therefore x + y = 9$
