## 문제
$\lim_{k \to +0} \frac{\overline{OA} - \overline{AC}}{\overline{OB} - \overline{BC}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{5}$   ② $\frac{1}{4}$   ③ $\frac{1}{3}$   ④ $\frac{1}{2}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
$$\lim _{k \to +0} \frac{\overline{OA} - \overline{AC}}{\overline{OB} - \overline{BC}} = \lim _{k \to +0} \frac{\sqrt{k^{2}+9k}-3\sqrt{k}}{\sqrt{k^{2}+k}-\sqrt{k}}$$

$$= \lim _{k \to +0} \frac{\sqrt{k+9}-3}{\sqrt{k+1}-1} = \lim _{k \to +0} \frac{k(\sqrt{k+1}+1)}{k(\sqrt{k+9}+3)}$$

$$= \lim _{k \to +0} \frac{\sqrt{k+1}+1}{\sqrt{k+9}+3} = \frac{1}{3}$$

정답: ③
