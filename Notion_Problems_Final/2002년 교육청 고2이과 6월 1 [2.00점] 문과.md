## 문제
$\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{3+\sqrt{8}}}+\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{3-\sqrt{8}}}$ 를 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2\sqrt{2}$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $2+\sqrt{2}$

## 풀이
$\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{3+\sqrt{8}}}+\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{3-\sqrt{8}}}=\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{3+2\sqrt{2}}}+\frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{3-2\sqrt{2}}}$
$= \sqrt{2} \left( \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}+1} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}-1} \right)$
$= \sqrt{2}(\sqrt{2}-1+\sqrt{2}+1)$
$= 4$

정답: ④
