## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{2x}-1}{4x}$ 의 값은? 

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{3}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $\frac{2}{3}$   ④ $\frac{5}{6}$   ⑤ $1$

## 풀이
$$ \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{2x}-1}{4x} $$
$$ = \lim _{x \to 0} \left( \frac{e^{2x}-1}{2x} \times \frac{2x}{4x} \right) $$
$$ = \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{2x}-1}{2x} \times \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{2x}{4x} $$
$$ = 1 \times \frac{2}{4} $$
$$ = \frac{1}{2} $$

정답: ②
