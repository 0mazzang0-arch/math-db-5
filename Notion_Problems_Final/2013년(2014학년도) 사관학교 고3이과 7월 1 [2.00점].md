## 문제
$\log_{2}(4\sqrt{2}-\sqrt{10})-\log_{2}(4-\sqrt{5})$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{4}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $\frac{3}{4}$   ④ $1$   ⑤ $\frac{5}{4}$

## 풀이
$\log_{2}(4\sqrt{2}-\sqrt{10})-\log_{2}(4-\sqrt{5})$
$= \log_{2} \frac{4\sqrt{2}-\sqrt{10}}{4-\sqrt{5}}$
$= \log_{2} \frac{\sqrt{2}(4-\sqrt{5})}{4-\sqrt{5}}$
$= \log_{2} \sqrt{2}$
$= \log_{2} 2^{\frac{1}{2}}$
$= \frac{1}{2}$

[정답] ②
