## 문제
행렬 $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$로 나타내어지는 일차변환을 $f$라 하자. 실수 $a$, $b$와 $2 \times 1$ 행렬 $X = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 $f(aX) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ b \end{array} \right)$일 때, $a+b$의 값은?

## 보기
① $5$   ② $6$   ③ $7$   ④ $8$   ⑤ $9$

## 풀이
$f(aX) = af(X)$이므로 $f(aX) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ b \end{array} \right)$에서
$a \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ b \end{array} \right)$
$a \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ b \end{array} \right)$
$\left( \begin{array}{c} 2a \\ 3a \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ b \end{array} \right)$

따라서 $2a = 4$, $3a = b$이므로 
$a = 2$, $b = 6$
$\therefore a+b = 8$

정답: ④
