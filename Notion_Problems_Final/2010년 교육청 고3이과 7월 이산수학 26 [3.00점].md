## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x \sin x}+e^{x \sin 2 x}-2}{x \ln (1+x)}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x \sin x}+e^{x \sin 2 x}-2}{x \ln (1+x)} $$ 
$$ = \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x \sin x}-1}{x \sin x} \times \frac{\sin x}{x} \times \frac{x}{\ln (1+x)} + \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{e^{x \sin 2 x}-1}{x \sin 2 x} \times \frac{\sin 2 x}{2 x} \times 2 \times \frac{x}{\ln (1+x)} $$ 
$$ = 1+2=3 $$ 

정답: ③
