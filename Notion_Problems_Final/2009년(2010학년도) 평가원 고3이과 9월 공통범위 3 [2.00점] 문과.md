## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 $X + B = AB$를 만족시키는 행렬 $X$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -4 \\ 3 & -6 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 3 & -1 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$X + B = AB$에서
$X = AB - B = (A - E) B$

$A - E = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 3 \end{array} \right)$ 이므로

$X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 2 \\ 0 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 0 \\ 1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$
$X = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -4 \\ 3 & -6 \end{array} \right)$
