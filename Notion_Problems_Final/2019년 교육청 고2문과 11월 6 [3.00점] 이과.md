## 문제
두 함수 $f(x)$, $g(x)$가 $\lim_{x \to 2} f(x) = 1$, $\lim_{x \to 2} \{2f(x) + g(x)\} = 8$을 만족시킬 때, $\lim_{x \to 2} g(x)$의 값은?

## 보기
① $2$   ② $4$   ③ $6$   ④ $8$   ⑤ $10$

## 풀이
[정답] ③ 

$$\lim_{x \to 2} 2f(x)$$ 
$$= 2 \times \lim_{x \to 2} f(x)$$ 
$$= 2$$ 이므로 

$$\lim_{x \to 2} g(x)$$ 
$$= \lim_{x \to 2} \{2f(x) + g(x) - 2f(x)\}$$ 
$$= \lim_{x \to 2} \{2f(x) + g(x)\} - \lim_{x \to 2} 2f(x)$$ 
$$= 8 - 2$$ 
$$= 6$$
