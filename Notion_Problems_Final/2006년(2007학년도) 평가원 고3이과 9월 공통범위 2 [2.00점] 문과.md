## 문제
두 행렬 $A$, $B$가 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right)$ 일 때, 행렬 $(A+B)^{2}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} -1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$ ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right)$ ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 2 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$ ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$A+B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$

$\therefore (A+B)^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

정답은 ⑤이다.
