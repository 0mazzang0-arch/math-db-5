## 문제
$(3 \times 9^{\frac{1}{3}})^{\frac{3}{5}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\sqrt[3]{3}$   ② $\sqrt[3]{3^{2}}$   ③ $3$   ④ $\sqrt[3]{3^{4}}$   ⑤ $\sqrt[3]{3^{5}}$

## 풀이
$$(3 \times 9^{\frac{1}{3}})^{\frac{3}{5}}$$
$$= (3 \cdot 3^{\frac{2}{3}})^{\frac{3}{5}}$$
$$= (3^{1 + \frac{2}{3}})^{\frac{3}{5}}$$
$$= (3^{\frac{5}{3}})^{\frac{3}{5}}$$
$$= 3^{1} = 3$$

정답: ③
