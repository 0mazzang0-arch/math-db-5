## 문제
$\log_{2} \frac{8}{9} + \frac{1}{2} \log_{\sqrt{2}} 18$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ \log_{2} \frac{8}{9} + \frac{1}{2} \log_{\sqrt{2}} 18 $$
$$ = \log_{2} \frac{8}{9} + \log_{2} 18 $$
$$ = \log_{2} \left( \frac{8}{9} \times 18 \right) $$
$$ = \log_{2} 2^{4} $$
$$ = 4 $$

[정답] ④
