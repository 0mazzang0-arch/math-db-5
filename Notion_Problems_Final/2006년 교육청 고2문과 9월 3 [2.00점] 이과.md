## 문제
$\log_{2} \frac{3}{4} + 2 \log_{2} \sqrt{6} - \log_{2} 9$ 를 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $-2$   ② $-1$   ③ $1$   ④ $-2 + \log_{2} 3$   ⑤ $-1 + \log_{2} 3$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$ \log_{2} \frac{3}{4} + 2 \log_{2} \sqrt{6} - \log_{2} 9 = \log_{2} \frac{3}{4} + \log_{2} 6 - \log_{2} 9 $$

$$ = \log_{2} \left( \frac{3}{4} \times 6 \div 9 \right) = \log_{2} \frac{1}{2} = -1 $$
