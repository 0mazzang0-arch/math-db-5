## 문제
연립방정식 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -3 \\ -5 & 8 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 
의 해를 $x = \alpha$, $y = \beta$라 할 때, $\alpha + \beta$의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기

## 풀이
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -3 \\ -5 & 8 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 
에서 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -3 \\ -5 & 8 \end{array} \right)^{-1} \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \frac{1}{16 - 15} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 8 & 3 \\ 5 & 2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{c} 14 \\ 9 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$\therefore \alpha + \beta = 14 + 9 = 23$

[정답] $23$
