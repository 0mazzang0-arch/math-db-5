## 문제
두 행렬 $$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$, $$ B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -7 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$ 에 대하여 $$ A^{100} B $$ 의 모든 성분의 합을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A^{3} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -3 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \cdots $$
$$ A^{n} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -n \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A^{100} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -100 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A^{100} B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -100 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -7 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 93 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서, $$ A^{100} B $$ 의 모든 성분의 합은 $$ 1 + 93 + 0 + (-1) = 93 $$ 이다.

정답: $93$
