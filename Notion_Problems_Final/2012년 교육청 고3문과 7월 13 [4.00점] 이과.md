## 문제
다항함수 $f$($x$)가 $\lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\int _{1} ^{x} f(t) dt - f(x)}{x^{2} - 1} = 2$를 만족할 때, $f'(1)$의 값은?

## 보기
① $-4$   ② $-3$   ③ $-2$   ④ $-1$   ⑤ $0$

## 풀이
$\int _{1} ^{1} f(t) dt - f(1) = 0$이므로 
$f(1) = 0$이다.

$$ \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\int _{1} ^{x} f(t) dt - f(x)}{x^{2} - 1} $$
$$ = \lim _{x \to 1} \left( \frac{\int _{1} ^{x} f(t) dt}{x^{2} - 1} - \frac{f(x) - f(1)}{x^{2} - 1} \right) $$
$$ = \lim _{x \to 1} \left( \frac{\int _{1} ^{x} f(t) dt}{x - 1} \cdot \frac{1}{x + 1} - \frac{f(x) - f(1)}{x - 1} \cdot \frac{1}{x + 1} \right) $$
$$ = \frac{f(1)}{2} - \frac{f'(1)}{2} = 2 $$

$\therefore \frac{0}{2} - \frac{f'(1)}{2} = 2$
$- \frac{f'(1)}{2} = 2$
$f'(1) = -4$

정답: ①
