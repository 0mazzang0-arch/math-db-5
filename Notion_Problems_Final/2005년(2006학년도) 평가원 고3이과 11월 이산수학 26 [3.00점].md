## 문제
$\lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\sec 2\theta - 1}{\sec \theta - 1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
[정답] ④ 

$$ \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\frac{1}{\cos 2\theta} - 1}{\frac{1}{\cos \theta} - 1} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{1 - \cos 2\theta}{1 - \cos \theta} \cdot \frac{\cos \theta}{\cos 2\theta} $$ 
$$ = \lim_{\theta \to 0} \frac{\sin^2 2\theta}{\sin^2 \theta} \cdot \frac{\cos \theta}{\cos 2\theta} \times \frac{1 + \cos \theta}{1 + \cos 2\theta} $$ 
$$ = 4 $$
