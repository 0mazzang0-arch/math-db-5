## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 3 \\ 1 & -2 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $AB + A$의 ($1$, $2$) 성분은?

## 보기
① $4$   ② $5$   ③ $6$   ④ $7$   ⑤ $8$

## 풀이
$$ AB + A = A(B + E) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left\{ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 3 \\ 1 & -2 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \right\} $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 3 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 7 \\ 1 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 행렬 $AB + A$의 ($1$, $2$) 성분은 $7$이다.
