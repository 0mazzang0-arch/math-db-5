## 문제
$\log_{5}(6-\sqrt{11}) + \log_{5}(6+\sqrt{11})$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ \log_{5}(6-\sqrt{11}) + \log_{5}(6+\sqrt{11}) $$
$$ = \log_{5}(6-\sqrt{11})(6+\sqrt{11}) $$
$$ = \log_{5}\{6^{2}-(\sqrt{11})^{2}\} $$
$$ = \log_{5}(36-11) $$
$$ = \log_{5} 25 $$
$$ = \log_{5} 5^{2} $$
$$ = 2 \log_{5} 5 = 2 $$

정답: ②
