## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 2} \frac{3x-6}{\sqrt{x+2}-2}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $8$   ② $9$   ③ $10$   ④ $11$   ⑤ $12$

## 풀이
$\lim _{x \to 2} \frac{3x-6}{\sqrt{x+2}-2}$
$= \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{(3x-6)(\sqrt{x+2}+2)}{(\sqrt{x+2}-2)(\sqrt{x+2}+2)}$
$= \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{(3x-6)(\sqrt{x+2}+2)}{(x+2)-4}$
$= \lim _{x \to 2} \frac{3(x-2)(\sqrt{x+2}+2)}{x-2}$
$= \lim _{x \to 2} 3(\sqrt{x+2}+2)$
$= 3(\sqrt{2+2}+2)$
$= 3(2+2)$
$= 12$

정답: ⑤
