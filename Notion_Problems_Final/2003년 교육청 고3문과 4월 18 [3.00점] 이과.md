## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $A^{n}$ 의 $(1, 2)$ 성분은? (단, $A^{n+1} = A \cdot A^{n}$, $n$ 은 자연수이다.)

## 보기
① $2^{n+1}-2$   ② $2^{n+1}-1$   ③ $2^{n}-2$   ④ $2^{n}-1$   ⑤ $2^{n}$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ A^{2} = A \cdot A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1+2 \\ 0 & 2^{2} \end{array} \right) $$

$$ A^{3} = A \cdot A^{2} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1+2 \\ 0 & 2^{2} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1+2+2^{2} \\ 0 & 2^{3} \end{array} \right) $$

$$ \cdots $$

$$ A^{n} = A \cdot A^{n-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1+2+2^{2} + \cdots + 2^{n-1} \\ 0 & 2^{n} \end{array} \right) $$

그러므로 $A^{n}$ 의 $(1, 2)$ 성분은 

$$ 1+2+2^{2}+ \cdots +2^{n-1} = \frac{1 \cdot (2^{n}-1)}{2-1} = 2^{n}-1 $$
