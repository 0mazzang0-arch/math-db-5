## 문제
$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+3 x)}{e^{2 x}-1}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{2}$   ② $\frac{3}{4}$   ③ $1$   ④ $\frac{5}{4}$   ⑤ $\frac{3}{2}$

## 풀이
$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+3 x)}{e^{2 x}-1}$
$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left( \frac{2 x}{e^{2 x}-1} \times \frac{\ln (1+3 x)}{3 x} \times \frac{3}{2} \right)$
$= 1 \times 1 \times \frac{3}{2}$
$= \frac{3}{2}$
