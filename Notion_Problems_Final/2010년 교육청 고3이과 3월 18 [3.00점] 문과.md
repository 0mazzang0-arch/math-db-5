## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 
$$ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \end{array} \right) , A^{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ 7 \end{array} \right) $$
일 때, $abcd$ 의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2a+3b \\ 2c+3d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \end{array} \right)$ 에서
$2a+3b=3$, $2c+3d=4$이다.

$A^{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) = A \left\{ A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) \right\} = A \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \end{array} \right)$ 이므로
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3a+4b \\ 3c+4d \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ 7 \end{array} \right) $$
$3a+4b=5$, $3c+4d=7$이다.

연립방정식을 풀면
$2a+3b=3$ 와 $3a+4b=5$ 에서 $a=3$, $b=-1$
$2c+3d=4$ 와 $3c+4d=7$ 에서 $c=5$, $d=-2$
따라서 $abcd = 3 \times (-1) \times 5 \times (-2) = 30$ 이다.

정답: $30$
