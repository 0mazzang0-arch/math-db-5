## 문제
$4 \log_{2} \sqrt{2} + \frac{1}{2} \log_{2} 3 - \log_{2} \sqrt{6}$ 을 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{2}$   ② $\frac{1}{2} + \log_{2} 3$   ③ $-\frac{1}{2}$   ④ $-\frac{1}{2} + \log_{2} 3$   ⑤ $\frac{3}{2}$

## 풀이
$$ 4 \log_{2} \sqrt{2} + \frac{1}{2} \log_{2} 3 - \log_{2} \sqrt{6} = \log_{2} 4 + \log_{2} \sqrt{3} - \log_{2} \sqrt{6} $$
$$ = \log_{2} \frac{4 \sqrt{3}}{\sqrt{6}} = \log_{2} \frac{4}{\sqrt{2}} = \log_{2} 2 \sqrt{2} $$
$$ = \log_{2} 2^{\frac{3}{2}} = \frac{3}{2} $$

정답: ⑤
