## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 6 & 3 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -6 & 4 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $AB - BA$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right)$ ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -4 & -2 \end{array} \right)$ ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & -1 \\ 4 & 2 \end{array} \right)$ ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & 3 \\ -12 & -6 \end{array} \right)$ ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & -3 \\ 12 & -6 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ④ 

$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 6 & 3 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -6 & 4 \end{array} \right)$에서 

$AB - BA$ 
$= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 6 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -6 & 4 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -6 & 4 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 6 & 3 \end{array} \right)$ 
$= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} -6 & -3 \\ 12 & 6 \end{array} \right)$ 
$= \left( \begin{array}{cc} 6 & 3 \\ -12 & -6 \end{array} \right)$
