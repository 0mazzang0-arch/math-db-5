## 문제
일차변환 $f$에 의하여 점 $($ $1$ $, $ $0$ $)$은 자기 자신으로 옮겨지고, 점 $($ $1$ $, $ $1$ $)$은 점 $($ $-1$ $, $ $-1$ $)$로 옮겨진다. 일차변환 $f$에 의하여 점 $($ $3$ $, $ $-4$ $)$가 점 $($ $a$ $, $ $b$ $)$로 옮겨질 때, $a$ $+$ $b$의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기

## 풀이
일차변환 $f$를 나타내는 행렬을 $$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} x & y \\ z & w \end{array} \right) $$라 하면
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} x & y \\ z & w \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} x & y \\ z & w \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$
점 $($ $3$ $, $ $-4$ $)$가 점 $($ $a$ $, $ $b$ $)$로 옮겨지므로
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} x & y \\ z & w \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} a \\ b \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -2 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ -4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} a \\ b \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} 11 \\ 4 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} a \\ b \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 $a$ $=$ $11$, $b$ $=$ $4$이므로
$\therefore$ $a$ $+$ $b$ $=$ $15$
