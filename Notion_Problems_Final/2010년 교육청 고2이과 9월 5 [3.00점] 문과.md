## 문제
이차정사각행렬 $A$ 에 대하여 $A^{2}=2 A-E$, $A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right)$ 를 만족할 때, 행렬 $A^{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right)$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 3 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 4 \\ 1 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ③ 

$$ A^{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = (2 A-E) \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = 2 A \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 4 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) $$
