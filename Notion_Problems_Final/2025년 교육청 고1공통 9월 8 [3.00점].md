## 문제
행렬 $$ A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) $$ 에 대하여 행렬 $$ A^{2} + A^{3} $$ 의 모든 성분의 합은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ A^{2} = AA = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & -4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ A^{3} = A^{2}A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & -4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & -2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -5 & -6 \\ 6 & 7 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ A^{2} + A^{3} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -3 & -4 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} -5 & -6 \\ 6 & 7 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -8 & -10 \\ 10 & 12 \end{array} \right) $$

따라서 행렬 $$ A^{2} + A^{3} $$ 의 모든 성분의 합은
$$ (-8) + (-10) + 10 + 12 = 4 $$

정답: ④
