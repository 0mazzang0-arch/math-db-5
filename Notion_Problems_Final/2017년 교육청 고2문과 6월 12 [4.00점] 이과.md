## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \left( \frac{1}{1 \times 3} + \frac{1}{2 \times 4} + \cdots + \frac{1}{n \times (n+2)} \right)$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{3}{8}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $\frac{5}{8}$   ④ $\frac{3}{4}$   ⑤ $\frac{7}{8}$

## 풀이
정답 ④

$$\lim _{n \to \infty} \left( \frac{1}{1 \times 3} + \frac{1}{2 \times 4} + \cdots + \frac{1}{n \times (n+2)} \right)$$

$$= \frac{1}{2} \lim _{n \to \infty} \left\{ \left( 1 - \frac{1}{3} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{4} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{n-1} - \frac{1}{n+1} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{n+2} \right) \right\}$$

$$= \frac{1}{2} \lim _{n \to \infty} \left( 1 + \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{n+1} - \frac{1}{n+2} \right)$$

$$= \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{3}{2} = \frac{3}{4}$$
