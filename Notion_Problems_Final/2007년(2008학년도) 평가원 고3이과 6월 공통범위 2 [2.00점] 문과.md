## 문제
두 행렬 $X$, $Y$에 대하여 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} X+Y = \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \end{array} \right) $$, $$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} Y = \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \end{array} \right) $$
일 때, $2X$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -4 \\ 4 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & -2 \\ 0 & 6 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ -2 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ -6 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ 0 & -4 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ⑤

$X$
$= X+Y-Y$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) $$
$\therefore 2X =$ $$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ 0 & -4 \end{array} \right) $$
