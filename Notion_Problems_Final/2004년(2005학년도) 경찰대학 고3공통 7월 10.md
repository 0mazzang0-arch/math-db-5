## 문제
$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right)$ , $A^{2004} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$ 일 때, $a+b+c+d$ 는? 

## 보기
① $2^{683}$   ② $2^{1935}$   ③ $2^{2898}$   ④ $2^{4009}$   ⑤ $2^{6404}$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$ A^{n}= \left( \begin{array}{cc} a_{n} & b_{n} \\ c_{n} & d_{n} \end{array} \right) $$ 
라 하면 
$$ A^{n+1}=A^{n} \cdot A $$ 이므로 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a_{n+1} & b_{n+1} \\ c_{n+1} & d_{n+1} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} a_{n} & b_{n} \\ c_{n} & d_{n} \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 a_{n}+b_{n} & a_{n}+3 b_{n} \\ 3 c_{n}+d_{n} & c_{n}+3 d_{n} \end{array} \right) $$ 
행렬의 상등에 의해 
$$ a_{n+1}=3 a_{n}+b_{n} \dots\dots ① $$
$$ b_{n+1}=a_{n}+3 b_{n} \dots\dots ② $$
$$ c_{n+1}=3 c_{n}+d_{n} \dots\dots ③ $$
$$ d_{n+1}=c_{n}+3 d_{n} \dots\dots ④ $$
식 ①과 ②, 식 ③과 ④를 더하면 
$$ a_{n}+b_{n}=4 \cdot 4^{n-1}=4^{n} $$ 
$$ c_{n}+d_{n}=4^{n} $$ 
따라서, 
$$ a_{2004}+b_{2004}+c_{2004}+d_{2004}=4^{2004}+4^{2004}=2 \cdot 4^{2004} = 2^{4009} $$
