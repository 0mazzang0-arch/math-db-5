## 문제
$\sqrt{2} \times \sqrt[3]{4} \div \sqrt[6]{4}$ 를 계산하면?

## 보기
① $\sqrt[3]{2^{5}}$   ② $\sqrt[3]{2^{7}}$   ③ $\sqrt[6]{2^{5}}$   ④ $\sqrt[6]{2^{7}}$   ⑤ $\sqrt[8]{2^{9}}$

## 풀이
$$ \sqrt{2} \times \sqrt[3]{4} \div \sqrt[6]{4} = 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 4^{\frac{1}{3}} \div 4^{\frac{1}{6}} = 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{2}{6}} $$
$$ = 2^{\frac{1}{2} + \frac{2}{3} - \frac{1}{3}} = 2^{\frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3}} = 2^{\frac{5}{6}} = \sqrt[6]{2^{5}} $$
정답은 ③이다.
