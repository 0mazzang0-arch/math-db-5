## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ x & y \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 $A B A^{-1} = B$가 성립할 때, $x y$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$A B A^{-1} = B$에서 $A B = B A$이므로 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ x & y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ x & y \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 + 2 x & 1 + 2 y \\ 2 + 3 x & 2 + 3 y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 5 \\ x + 2 y & 2 x + 3 y \end{array} \right) $$ 
두 행렬이 서로 같을 조건에 의해 
$1 + 2 x = 3$, $1 + 2 y = 5$, 
$2 + 3 x = x + 2 y$, $2 + 3 y = 2 x + 3 y$ 
따라서 $x = 1$, $y = 2$ 
$\therefore x y = 2$

정답: ②
