## 문제
두 이차정사각행렬 $A$, $B$에 대하여
$$ \begin{array}{c} A+B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 5 \\ -4 & 1 \end{array} \right) , \space A-B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 5 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
일 때, 행렬 $A$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 5 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$  ② $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 4 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$  ③ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 5 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$  ④ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 5 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$  ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 4 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ \begin{array}{l} A = \frac{1}{2} \{ (A+B) + (A-B) \} \\ A = \frac{1}{2} \left\{ \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & 5 \\ -4 & 1 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 4 & 5 \\ 2 & 3 \end{array} \right) \right\} \\ A = \frac{1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{rr} 6 & 10 \\ -2 & 4 \end{array} \right) \\ A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 5 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
따라서 정답은 ③이다.
