## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 $2A + X = AB$를 만족시키는 행렬 $X$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 5 \\ 3 & -1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 5 \\ 7 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 7 \\ 4 & 5 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 6 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$2A + X = AB$ 에서 
$X = AB - 2A$ 
$X = A(B - 2E)$ 
$$ \begin{array}{rcl} X & = & \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} -1 & 2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right) \\ & = & \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 4 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
따라서 만족시키는 행렬 $X$는 ②이다.
