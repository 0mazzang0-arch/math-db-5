## 문제
$\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $-2\sqrt{3}$   ② $-2\sqrt{2}$   ③ $0$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $2\sqrt{3}$

## 풀이
정답 ⑤

$$\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2}}$$
$$= \sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2} + \frac{\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{2}}{(\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2})(\sqrt{3} + \sqrt{2})}$$
$$= \sqrt{3} - \sqrt{2} + \sqrt{3} + \sqrt{2}$$
$$= 2\sqrt{3}$$
