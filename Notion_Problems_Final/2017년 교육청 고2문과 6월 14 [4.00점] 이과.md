## 문제
$\sum _{k=1} ^{10} \frac{k ^{3}}{k+1} + \sum _{k=1} ^{10} \frac{1}{k+1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $340$   ② $360$   ③ $380$   ④ $400$   ⑤ $420$

## 풀이
$$ \sum _{k=1} ^{10} \frac{k ^{3}}{k+1} + \sum _{k=1} ^{10} \frac{1}{k+1} $$ 
$$ = \sum _{k=1} ^{10} \frac{k ^{3} +1}{k+1} $$ 
$$ = \sum _{k=1} ^{10} \frac{(k+1)(k ^{2} -k+1)}{k+1} $$ 
$$ = \sum _{k=1} ^{10} (k ^{2} -k+1) $$ 
$$ = \sum _{k=1} ^{10} k ^{2} - \sum _{k=1} ^{10} k+ \sum _{k=1} ^{10} 1 $$ 
$$ = \frac{10 \times 11 \times 21}{6} - \frac{10 \times 11}{2} + 1 \times 10 $$ 
$$ = 385-55+10 $$ 
$$ = 340 $$ 
정답은 ①입니다.
