## 문제
함수 $f(x)$에 대하여 $\lim_{x \to 0} f(x) \left( 1 - \cos \frac{x}{2} \right) = 1$일 때, $\lim_{x \to 0} x^{2} f(x)$의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$\lim_{x \to 0} x^{2} f(x) = \lim_{x \to 0} \left\{ f(x) \left( 1 - \cos \frac{x}{2} \right) \times \frac{x^{2}}{1 - \cos \frac{x}{2}} \right\}$
$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left\{ f(x) \left( 1 - \cos \frac{x}{2} \right) \times \frac{x^{2} \left( 1 + \cos \frac{x}{2} \right)}{\left( 1 - \cos \frac{x}{2} \right) \left( 1 + \cos \frac{x}{2} \right)} \right\}$
$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left\{ f(x) \left( 1 - \cos \frac{x}{2} \right) \times \frac{\frac{x^{2}}{4}}{\sin^{2} \frac{x}{2}} \times 4 \times \left( 1 + \cos \frac{x}{2} \right) \right\}$
$= 1 \times 1 \times 4 \times 2 = 8$

정답: $8$
