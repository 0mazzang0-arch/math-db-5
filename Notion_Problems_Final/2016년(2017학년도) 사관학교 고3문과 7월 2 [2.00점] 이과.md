## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3^{n} + 2^{n+1}}{3^{n+1} - 2^{n}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{3}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $1$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $3$

## 풀이
$$ \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{3^{n} + 2^{n+1}}{3^{n+1} - 2^{n}} = \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1+2 \left( \frac{2}{3} \right)^{n}}{3- \left( \frac{2}{3} \right)^{n}} = \frac{1}{3} $$
정답: ①
