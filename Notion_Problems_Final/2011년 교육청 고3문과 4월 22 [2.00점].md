## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{3}{\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} - n}$ 의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기

## 풀이
$$ \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{3}{\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} - n} $$
$$ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{3(\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} + n)}{(\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} - n)(\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} + n)} $$
$$ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{3(\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} + n)}{(n^{2} + 3n + 2) - n^{2}} $$
$$ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{3(\sqrt{n^{2} + 3n + 2} + n)}{3n + 2} $$
$$ = \lim _{n \to \infty} \frac{3(\sqrt{1 + \frac{3}{n} + \frac{2}{n^{2}}} + 1)}{3 + \frac{2}{n}} $$
$$ = \frac{3(1 + 1)}{3} = 2 $$

정답: $2$
