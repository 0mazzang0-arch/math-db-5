## 문제
$4^{\frac{1}{4}} \times 2^{\frac{1}{2}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
$$ 4^{\frac{1}{4}} \times 2^{\frac{1}{2}} $$
$$ = (2^{2})^{\frac{1}{4}} \times 2^{\frac{1}{2}} $$
$$ = 2^{2 \times \frac{1}{4}} \times 2^{\frac{1}{2}} $$
$$ = 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 2^{\frac{1}{2}} $$
$$ = 2^{\frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2}} $$
$$ = 2^{1} = 2 $$

따라서 정답은 ②입니다.
