## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $AB + B^{-1}$ 의 모든 성분의 합은?

## 보기
① $8$   ② $7$   ③ $6$   ④ $5$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$ AB = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ B^{-1} = \frac{1}{2 \times 1 - 1 \times 1} \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & -1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & -1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ \therefore AB + B^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 1 & 2 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{rr} 2 & -1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{rr} 3 & 1 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$

$$ \therefore $$ 모든 성분의 합
$$ = 3 + 1 + (-1) + 2 = 5 $$
