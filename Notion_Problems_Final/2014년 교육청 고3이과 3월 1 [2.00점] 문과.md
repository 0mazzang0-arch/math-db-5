## 문제
$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right)$, $2 A + B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 3 & -1 \end{array} \right)$ 일 때, 행렬 $B$ 는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 2 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 3 & -1 \end{array} \right) - 2 A $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 3 & -1 \end{array} \right) - 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 5 & 2 \\ 3 & -1 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 0 \\ 0 & -2 \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
