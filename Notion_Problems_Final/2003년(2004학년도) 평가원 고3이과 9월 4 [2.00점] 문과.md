## 문제
정적분 $\int_{1}^{e} (4x \ln x) dx$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $e^{2}-1$   ② $e^{2}+1$   ③ $e^{2}+2$   ④ $e^{2}+e-1$   ⑤ $e^{2}+2 e+2$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$\int_{1}^{e} (4x \ln x) dx = 4 \int_{1}^{e} (x \ln x) dx$ 

$= 4 \left\{ \left[ \frac{1}{2} x^2 \ln x \right]_{1}^{e} - \int_{1}^{e} \left( \frac{1}{x} \cdot \frac{1}{2} x^2 \right) dx \right\}$ 

$= 4 \left\{ \frac{1}{2} e^2 - \left[ \frac{1}{4} x^2 \right]_{1}^{e} \right\}$ 

$= e^2 + 1$
