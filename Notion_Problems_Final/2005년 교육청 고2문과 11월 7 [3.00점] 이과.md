## 문제
$x$, $y$에 대한 연립일차방정식 $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} a x+b y=s \\ c x+d y=t \end{array} \right.$ 와 $s$, $t$에 대한 연립일차방정식 $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} e s+f t=3 \\ g s+h t=2 \end{array} \right.$ 가 있다. 행렬 $\left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right)$ 의 역행렬이 $\left( \begin{array}{cc} e & f \\ g & h \end{array} \right)$ 일 때, $x+y$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
[정답] ⑤ 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} s \\ t \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} e & f \\ g & h \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} s \\ t \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 이므로 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} e & f \\ g & h \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} a & b \\ c & d \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \therefore \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 3 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) $$ 

따라서, $x+y=5$ 이다.
