## 문제
무한수열 $\{a_n\}$을
$$ a_n = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} 0 & (n = 3k - 2) \\ 1 & (n = 3k - 1) \\ 2 & (n = 3k) \end{array} \right. $$
(단, $k$는 자연수)로 정의할 때, $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{a_n}{4^n}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{3}{4}$   ② $\frac{2}{21}$   ③ $\frac{13}{32}$   ④ $\frac{17}{54}$   ⑤ $\frac{29}{63}$

## 풀이
[정답] ②

$$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{a_n}{4^n} = \frac{0}{4} + \frac{1}{4^2} + \frac{2}{4^3} + \frac{0}{4^4} + \frac{1}{4^5} + \frac{2}{4^6} + \dots $$
$$ = \left( \frac{1}{4^2} + \frac{1}{4^5} + \frac{1}{4^8} + \dots \right) + \left( \frac{2}{4^3} + \frac{2}{4^6} + \frac{2}{4^9} + \dots \right) $$
$$ = \frac{\frac{1}{4^2}}{1 - \frac{1}{4^3}} + \frac{\frac{2}{4^3}}{1 - \frac{1}{4^3}} = \frac{\frac{1}{16}}{\frac{63}{64}} + \frac{\frac{2}{64}}{\frac{63}{64}} = \frac{4}{63} + \frac{2}{63} = \frac{2}{21} $$
