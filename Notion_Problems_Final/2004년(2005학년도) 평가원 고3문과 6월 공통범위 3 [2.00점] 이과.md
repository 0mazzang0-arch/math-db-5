## 문제
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{n}{\sqrt{2n^{2}-n} + \sqrt{n^{2}-1}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\sqrt{2}-1$   ② $1$   ③ $\sqrt{2}$   ④ $2$   ⑤ $\sqrt{2}+1$

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{n}{\sqrt{2n^{2}-n} + \sqrt{n^{2}-1}}$

$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\frac{1}{n} \sqrt{2n^{2}-n} + \frac{1}{n} \sqrt{n^{2}-1}}$

$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2-\frac{1}{n}} + \sqrt{1-\frac{1}{n^{2}}}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}+1} = \sqrt{2}-1$

정답: ①
