## 문제
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 행렬 $A - A^{-1}$ 은?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 1 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 2 \\ 1 & 4 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 4 \\ 2 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 0 \\ 0 & 4 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right)$ 의 역행렬 $A^{-1}$ 을 구하면
$$ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{1 \times 3 - 2 \times 1} \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 
$$ A - A^{-1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -2 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} -2 & 4 \\ 2 & 2 \end{array} \right) $$
정답은 ④이다.
