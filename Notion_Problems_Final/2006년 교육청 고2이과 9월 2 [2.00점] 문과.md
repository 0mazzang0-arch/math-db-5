## 문제
세 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ -1 & 3 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \right)$, $C = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right)$에 대하여 행렬 $(A+B)C$는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 3 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ 3 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ 8 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 10 \\ 3 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{c} 5 \\ 10 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$$ A+B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ -1 & 3 \end{array} \right) + \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & 1 \\ 2 & -2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 3 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로
$$ (A+B)C = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 4 & 3 \\ 1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 2 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 10 \\ 3 \end{array} \right) $$
따라서 정답은 ④이다.
