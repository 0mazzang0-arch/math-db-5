## 문제
$x$, $y$에 대한 연립일차방정식 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 4a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} ay \\ 2x-y \end{array} \right) $$
가 $x=0$, $y=0$ 이외의 해를 가질 때, 상수 $a$의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기
① $1$   ② $2$   ③ $3$   ④ $4$   ⑤ $5$

## 풀이
주어진 식을 정리하면
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 4a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & a \\ 2 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & 4a \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & a \\ 2 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1-0 & -1-a \\ -1-2 & 4a-(-1) \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1-a \\ -3 & 4a+1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} x \\ y \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) \cdots\cdots ㉠ $$
따라서, 행렬 $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1-a \\ -3 & 4a+1 \end{array} \right)$의 역행렬이 존재하지 않아야 ㉠은 $x=0$, $y=0$ 이외의 해를 가지므로
$1 \times (4a+1) - (-1-a)(-3) = 0$
$4a+1 - (3+3a) = 0$
$a-2=0$
$\therefore a=2$

정답: ②
