## 문제
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{\sqrt{4 n^{2}-(n+k)^{2}}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{\pi}{12}$   ② $\frac{\pi}{6}$   ③ $\frac{\pi}{4}$   ④ $\frac{\pi}{3}$   ⑤ $\frac{\pi}{2}$

## 풀이
$\lim _{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{\sqrt{4 n^{2}-(n+k)^{2}}}$
$= \int_{1}^{2} \frac{1}{\sqrt{4-x^{2}}} d x = \int_{\frac{\pi}{6}}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} d \theta = \frac{\pi}{3}$
