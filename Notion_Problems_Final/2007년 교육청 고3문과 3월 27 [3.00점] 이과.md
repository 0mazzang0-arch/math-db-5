## 문제
집합 $X = \left\{ \left. \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & x \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \right| x \text{ 는 실수} \right\}$ 에 대하여 $A \in X$, $B \in X$ 일 때, <보기>에서 옳은 것만을 있는 대로 고른 것은?

ㄱ. $AB \in X$
ㄴ. $A^2 - B^2 = (A + B)(A - B)$
ㄷ. $(A + B)^2 = 4AB$

## 보기
① ㄱ   ② ㄴ   ③ ㄱ, ㄴ   ④ ㄴ, ㄷ   ⑤ ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄷ

## 풀이
[정답] ⑤ 

$A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right)$ ($a$, $b$ 는 실수)이라 하면 

ㄱ. 
$$ AB = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a + b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 $AB \in X$ (참) 

ㄴ. 
$$ BA = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a + b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 $AB = BA$ 
$\therefore A^2 - B^2 = (A + B)(A - B)$ (참) 

ㄷ. 
$$ A^2 = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2a \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ B^2 = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
이므로 
$$ A^2 + B^2 = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 2(a + b) \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right) = 2 \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & a + b \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = 2AB $$
$\therefore (A + B)^2 = A^2 + B^2 + 2AB = 2AB + 2AB = 4AB$ (참)
