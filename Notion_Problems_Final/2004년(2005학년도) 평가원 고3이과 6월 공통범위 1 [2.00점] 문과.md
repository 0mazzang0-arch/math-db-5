## 문제
$25^{-\frac{3}{2}} \times 100^{\frac{3}{2}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $2$   ② $4$   ③ $6$   ④ $8$   ⑤ $10$

## 풀이
정답: ④

$$
\begin{aligned}
25^{-\frac{3}{2}} \times 100^{\frac{3}{2}} &= \left( \frac{1}{25} \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} \times 100^{\frac{3}{2}} \\
&= \left( \frac{1}{25} \times 100 \right)^{\frac{3}{2}} \\
&= 4^{\frac{3}{2}} \\
&= (2^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}} \\
&= 2^{3} \\
&= 8
\end{aligned}
$$
