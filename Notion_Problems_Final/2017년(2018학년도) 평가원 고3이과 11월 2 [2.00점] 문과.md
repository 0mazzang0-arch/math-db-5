## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1+5x)}{e^{2x}-1}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\frac{3}{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $\frac{5}{2}$   ⑤ $3$

## 풀이
$\lim _{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1+5x)}{e^{2x}-1}$
$= \frac{5}{2} \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1+5x)}{5x} \times \lim _{x \to 0} \frac{2x}{e^{2x}-1}$
$= \frac{5}{2} \times 1 \times 1$
$= \frac{5}{2}$

정답: ④
