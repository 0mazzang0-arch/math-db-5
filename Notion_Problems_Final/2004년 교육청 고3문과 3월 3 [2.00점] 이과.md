## 문제
두 행렬 $A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$, $B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$ 에 대하여 $AB - A$ 는?

## 보기
① $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ② $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ③ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right)$   ④ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{array} \right)$   ⑤ $\left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 2 & 0 \end{array} \right)$

## 풀이
$AB - A = A(B - E)$
$E$ 를 단위행렬이라 하면,
$B - E = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 1 \\ -1 & 0 \end{array} \right) - \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & -1 \end{array} \right)$
따라서,
$$ \begin{array}{ll} AB - A & = A(B - E) \\ & = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & -1 \\ -1 & -1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ -1 & -1 \end{array} \right) \\ & = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \end{array} $$
정답은 ① 이다.
