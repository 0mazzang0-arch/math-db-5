## 문제
$\sqrt[3]{24} \times 3^{\frac{2}{3}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $6$   ② $7$   ③ $8$   ④ $9$   ⑤ $10$

## 풀이
$\sqrt[3]{24} \times 3^{\frac{2}{3}}$
$= (2^{3} \times 3)^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 3^{\frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2 \times 3^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 3^{\frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2 \times 3^{\frac{1}{3} + \frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2 \times 3^{1}$
$= 6$

정답 ①
