## 문제
두 연립방정식 
$$ \begin{cases} x_{1} = 2 y_{2} + y_{3} \\ x_{2} = y_{1} + 2 y_{2} \end{cases} $$, 
$$ \begin{cases} y_{1} = z_{1} + 2 z_{2} \\ y_{2} = 2 z_{1} - z_{2} \\ y_{3} = - z_{1} + z_{2} \end{cases} $$
을 행렬을 이용하여 
$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x_{1} \\ x_{2} \end{array} \right) = A \left( \begin{array}{c} z_{1} \\ z_{2} \end{array} \right) $$
로 표현할 때, 행렬 $A$는?

## 보기
① $ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ -5 & 3 \end{array} \right) $   ② $ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 5 & 0 \end{array} \right) $   ③ $ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{array} \right) $   ④ $ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ -1 & 2 \end{array} \right) $   ⑤ $ \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 5 \end{array} \right) $

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x_{1} \\ x_{2} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 0 & 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} y_{1} \\ y_{2} \\ y_{3} \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} y_{1} \\ y_{2} \\ y_{3} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 2 & -1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} z_{1} \\ z_{2} \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x_{1} \\ x_{2} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{ccc} 0 & 2 & 1 \\ 1 & 2 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 2 \\ 2 & -1 \\ -1 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} z_{1} \\ z_{2} \end{array} \right) $$ 
$$ = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 5 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} z_{1} \\ z_{2} \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \therefore A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 5 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$ 

(다른 풀이) 
대입하여 정리하면 

$$ x_{1} = 2 ( 2 z_{1} - z_{2} ) + ( - z_{1} + z_{2} ) = 3 z_{1} - z_{2} $$ 

$$ x_{2} = ( z_{1} + 2 z_{2} ) + 2 ( 2 z_{1} - z_{2} ) = 5 z_{1} $$ 

$$ \left( \begin{array}{c} x_{1} \\ x_{2} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 5 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{c} z_{1} \\ z_{2} \end{array} \right) $$ 

$$ \therefore A = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 3 & -1 \\ 5 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
