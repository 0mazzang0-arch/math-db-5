## 문제
$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+3x) + 9x}{2x}$ 의 값을 구하시오.

## 풀이
$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+3x) + 9x}{2x}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left\{ \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{1}{3x} \ln (1+3x) + \frac{9x}{2x} \right\}$$
$$= \lim_{x \to 0} \left\{ \frac{3}{2} \times \frac{1}{3x} \ln (1+3x) + \frac{9}{2} \right\}$$
$$= \frac{3}{2} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+3x)}{3x} + \frac{9}{2}$$
이때 $3x = t$ 로 놓으면 $x \to 0$ 일 때 $t \to 0$ 이므로
$$\frac{3}{2} \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+3x)}{3x} + \frac{9}{2}$$
$$= \frac{3}{2} \lim_{t \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+t)}{t} + \frac{9}{2}$$
$$= \frac{3}{2} \times 1 + \frac{9}{2}$$
$$= 6$$
정답은 $6$ 이다.
