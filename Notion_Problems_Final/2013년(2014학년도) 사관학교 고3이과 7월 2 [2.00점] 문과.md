## 문제
$\lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\ln x}{x^{3}-1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{3}$   ② $\frac{1}{2}$   ③ $1$   ④ $\frac{3}{2}$   ⑤ $2$

## 풀이
[정답] ①

$f(x) = \ln x$ 라 할 때,
$f'(x) = \frac{1}{x}$ 이므로

$\lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\ln x}{x^{3}-1}$
$= \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\ln x}{x-1} \cdot \frac{1}{x^{2}+x+1}$
$= \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{f(x)-f(1)}{x-1} \cdot \frac{1}{x^{2}+x+1}$
$= f'(1) \cdot \frac{1}{3}$
$= \frac{1}{3}$

[다른 풀이]
$\lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\ln x}{x^{3}-1}$
$= \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{\ln x}{x-1} \cdot \frac{1}{x^{2}+x+1}$
$= \lim _{t \to 0} \frac{\ln (1+t)}{t} \cdot \lim _{x \to 1} \frac{1}{x^{2}+x+1}$
$= 1 \cdot \frac{1}{3} = \frac{1}{3}$
