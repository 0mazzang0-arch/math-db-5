## 문제
$8^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{2}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{3}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $2^3$   ② $2^4$   ③ $2^5$   ④ $2^6$   ⑤ $2^7$

## 풀이
[정답] ③

$$8^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{2}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{3}} = 2^{4} \times 2^{\frac{4}{3}} \div 2^{\frac{1}{3}} = 2^{4 + \frac{4}{3} - \frac{1}{3}} = 2^{5}$$
