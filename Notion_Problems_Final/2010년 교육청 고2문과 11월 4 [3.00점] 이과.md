## 문제
$a = \sqrt[4]{3}$, $b = \sqrt[6]{4}$일 때, $\sqrt[12]{6}$을 $a$, $b$로 나타낸 것은?

## 보기
① $a^{\frac{1}{2}} b^{\frac{1}{4}}$   ② $a^{\frac{1}{3}} b^{\frac{1}{4}}$   ③ $a^{\frac{1}{3}} b^{\frac{1}{6}}$   ④ $a^{\frac{1}{4}} b^{\frac{1}{6}}$   ⑤ $a^{\frac{1}{6}} b^{\frac{1}{12}}$

## 풀이
$a = \sqrt[4]{3} = 3^{\frac{1}{4}}$, 
$b = \sqrt[6]{4} = (2^{2})^{\frac{1}{6}} = 2^{\frac{1}{3}}$이므로 

$\sqrt[12]{6} = 6^{\frac{1}{12}} = (2 \times 3)^{\frac{1}{12}} = 2^{\frac{1}{12}} \times 3^{\frac{1}{12}}$
$= (2^{\frac{1}{3}})^{\frac{1}{4}} \times (3^{\frac{1}{4}})^{\frac{1}{3}} = b^{\frac{1}{4}} \times a^{\frac{1}{3}} = a^{\frac{1}{3}} b^{\frac{1}{4}}$

정답: ②
