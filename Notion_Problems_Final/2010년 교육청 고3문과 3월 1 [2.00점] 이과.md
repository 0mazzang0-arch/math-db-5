## 문제
$2^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times 10^{\frac{4}{3}}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $2$   ② $5$   ③ $10$   ④ $20$   ⑤ $40$

## 풀이
$2^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times 10^{\frac{4}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times (2 \times 5)^{\frac{4}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{2}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{4}{3}} \times 5^{\frac{4}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{2}{3} + \frac{4}{3}} \times 5^{-\frac{1}{3} + \frac{4}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{6}{3}} \times 5^{\frac{3}{3}}$
$= 2^{2} \times 5^{1}$
$= 4 \times 5 = 20$
