## 문제
$\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2+\sqrt{2}}+\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2-\sqrt{2}}$ 를 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $2\sqrt{2}$   ② $4$   ③ $4\sqrt{2}$   ④ $6\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $8$

## 풀이
$($ 주어진 식 $)$
$$= \frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{2}(\sqrt{2}+1)} + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{\sqrt{2}(\sqrt{2}-1)}$$
$$= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}+1} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}-1}$$
$$= \frac{\sqrt{2}-1}{(\sqrt{2}+1)(\sqrt{2}-1)} + \frac{\sqrt{2}+1}{(\sqrt{2}-1)(\sqrt{2}+1)}$$
$$= (\sqrt{2}-1) + (\sqrt{2}+1)$$
$$= 2\sqrt{2}$$

정답: ①
