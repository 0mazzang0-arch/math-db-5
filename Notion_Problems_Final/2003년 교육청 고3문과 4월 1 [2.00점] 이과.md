## 문제
$4 \log _{2} \sqrt{2} + \frac{1}{2} \log _{2} 3 - \log _{2} \sqrt{6}$ 을 간단히 하면?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{2}$   ② $\frac{1}{2} + \log _{2} 3$   ③ $-\frac{1}{2}$   ④ $-\frac{1}{2} + \log _{2} 3$   ⑤ $\frac{3}{2}$

## 풀이
$$4 \log _{2} \sqrt{2} + \frac{1}{2} \log _{2} 3 - \log _{2} \sqrt{6} = \log _{2} 4 + \log _{2} \sqrt{3} - \log _{2} \sqrt{6}$$
$$= \log _{2} \frac{4 \sqrt{3}}{\sqrt{6}} = \log _{2} 2^{\frac{3}{2}} = \frac{3}{2}$$
