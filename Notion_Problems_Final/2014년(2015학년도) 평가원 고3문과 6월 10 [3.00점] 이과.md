## 문제
$\sum _{k=1} ^{n} \frac{4}{k(k+1)} = \frac{15}{4}$ 일 때, $n$의 값은?

## 보기
① $11$   ② $12$   ③ $13$   ④ $14$   ⑤ $15$

## 풀이
$$ \sum _{k=1} ^{n} \frac{4}{k(k+1)} $$
$$ =4 \sum _{k=1} ^{n} \left( \frac{1}{k} - \frac{1}{k+1} \right) $$
$$ =4 \left\{ \left( \frac{1}{1} - \frac{1}{2} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{3} \right) + \left( \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{4} \right) + \cdots + \left( \frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{n+1} \right) \right\} $$
$$ =4 \left( 1 - \frac{1}{n+1} \right) $$
$$ = \frac{4n}{n+1} $$

따라서 $\frac{4n}{n+1} = \frac{15}{4}$ 에서
$16n = 15n + 15$
$n = 15$
