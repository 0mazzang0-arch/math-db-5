## 문제
두 이차정사각행렬
$$ A_{1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) , B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
에 대하여
$$ A_{n+1} = A_{n} B \quad (n = 1, 2, 3, \cdots) $$
일 때, <보기>에서 옳은 것을 모두 고른 것은?

---
**<보기>**
ㄱ. $A_{2} = A_{5}$
ㄴ. $A_{2n+2} = A_{2n} A_{2n+2}$
ㄷ. $A_{2n+1} = A_{2n} A_{2n+1}$

## 보기
① $ㄱ$   ② $ㄴ$   ③ $ㄱ, ㄴ$   ④ $ㄴ, ㄷ$   ⑤ $ㄱ, ㄴ, ㄷ$

## 풀이
[정답] ④

$$ A_{1} = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A_{2} = A_{1} B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A_{3} = A_{2} B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A_{4} = A_{3} B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) $$
$$ A_{5} = A_{4} B = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{array} \right) \left( \begin{array}{cc} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{cc} 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 0 \end{array} \right) $$

ㄱ. $A_{2} \neq A_{5}$ 이므로 거짓이다.

ㄴ. $A_{2n+2} = A_{2}$와 같고, $A_{2n} \cdot A_{2n+2} = A_{2} \cdot A_{2} = A_{2}$와 같다.
$\therefore$ 참

ㄷ. $A_{2n+1} = A_{1}$과 같고, $A_{2n} \cdot A_{2n+1} = A_{2} \cdot A_{1} = A_{1}$과 같다.
$\therefore$ 참
