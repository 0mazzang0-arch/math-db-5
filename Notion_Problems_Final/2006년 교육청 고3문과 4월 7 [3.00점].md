## 문제
$\sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}}$, $\sqrt{2^{\sqrt{2}}}$, $(\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}}$ 의 대소 관계는?

## 보기
① $\sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2^{\sqrt{2}}} < (\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}}$
② $\sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2}^{\sqrt{2}} = (\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}}$
③ $\sqrt{2}^{\sqrt{2}} = (\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}}$
④ $\sqrt{2}^{\sqrt{2}} < (\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}}$
⑤ $(\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2}^{\sqrt{2}}$

## 풀이
[정답] ③ 

$\sqrt{2^{\sqrt{2}}} = 2^{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}$
$(\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}} = 2^{\frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}}$
$\sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}} = 2^{\frac{3}{4}}$
$\therefore \sqrt{2^{\sqrt{2}}} = (\sqrt{2})^{\sqrt{2}} < \sqrt{2 \sqrt{2}}$
