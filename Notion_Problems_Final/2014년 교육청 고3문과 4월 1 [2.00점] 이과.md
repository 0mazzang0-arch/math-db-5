## 문제
$2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 4^{\frac{3}{4}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $1$   ② $\sqrt{2}$   ③ $2$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$$2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 4^{\frac{3}{4}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times (2^{2})^{\frac{3}{4}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 2^{2 \times \frac{3}{4}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{1}{2}} \times 2^{\frac{3}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{1}{2} + \frac{3}{2}}$$
$$= 2^{\frac{4}{2}} = 2^{2} = 4$$

정답은 ⑤입니다.
