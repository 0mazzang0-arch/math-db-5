## 문제
$2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{1}{3}}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\sqrt{2}$   ② $2$   ③ $2\sqrt[3]{2}$   ④ $2\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $4$

## 풀이
$2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{1}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times (2^{2})^{\frac{1}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 2^{\frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2^{\frac{1}{3} + \frac{2}{3}}$
$= 2^{1} = 2$

[다른 풀이]
$2^{\frac{1}{3}} \times 4^{\frac{1}{3}}$
$= (2 \times 4)^{\frac{1}{3}} = 8^{\frac{1}{3}} = (2^{3})^{\frac{1}{3}} = 2$
