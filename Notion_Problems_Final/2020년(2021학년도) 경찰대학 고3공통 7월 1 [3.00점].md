## 문제
$\log_{3} (\log_{27} x) = \log_{27} (\log_{3} x)$가 성립할 때, $(\log_{3} x)^{2}$의 값은?

## 보기
① $\frac{1}{9}$   ② $\frac{1}{27}$   ③ $3$   ④ $9$   ⑤ $27$

## 풀이
$\log_{3} x = t$라 하면 
$\log_{27} x = \frac{1}{3} t$이므로 

$\log_{3} (\log_{27} x)$ 
$= \log_{3} \left( \frac{1}{3} t \right)$ 
$= \log_{27} \left( \frac{1}{3} t \right)^{3}$ 
$= \log_{27} \left( \frac{1}{27} t^{3} \right)$ 

$\log_{3} (\log_{27} x) = \log_{27} (\log_{3} x)$에서 
$\log_{27} \left( \frac{1}{27} t^{3} \right) = \log_{27} t$ 

$\therefore \frac{1}{27} t^{3} = t$ 
$t^{2} = 27$ 

따라서 
$(\log_{3} x)^{2} = 27$
