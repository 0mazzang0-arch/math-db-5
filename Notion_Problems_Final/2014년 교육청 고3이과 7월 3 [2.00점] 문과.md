## 문제
$\lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^{2} - 4}{\sqrt{x+2} - 2}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $12$   ② $16$   ③ $20$   ④ $24$   ⑤ $28$

## 풀이
[정답] ② 

$\lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^{2} - 4}{\sqrt{x+2} - 2}$ 에서 분모, 분자에 $(\sqrt{x+2} + 2)$ 를 곱하면 

$\lim_{x \to 2} \frac{(x+2)(x-2)(\sqrt{x+2} + 2)}{(\sqrt{x+2}-2)(\sqrt{x+2}+2)}$
$= \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{(x+2)(x-2)(\sqrt{x+2} + 2)}{x-2}$
$= \lim_{x \to 2} (x+2)(\sqrt{x+2} + 2)$
$= (2+2)(\sqrt{2+2} + 2)$
$= 4 \times 4 = 16$
