## 문제
함수 $f(x) = x^3 + x$일 때, $\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} f\left(1 + \frac{2k}{n}\right)$의 값을 구하시오.

## 보기

## 풀이
$\lim_{n \to \infty} \frac{1}{n} \sum_{k=1}^{n} f\left(1 + \frac{2k}{n}\right)$
$= \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{k=1}^{n} f\left(1 + \frac{2k}{n}\right) \cdot \frac{2}{n} \cdot \frac{1}{2}$
$= \frac{1}{2} \int_{1}^{3} f(x) dx$
$= \frac{1}{2} \int_{1}^{3} (x^3 + x) dx$
$= \frac{1}{2} \left[ \frac{1}{4} x^4 + \frac{1}{2} x^2 \right]_{1}^{3}$
$= \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{81}{4} + \frac{9}{2} - \frac{1}{4} - \frac{1}{2} \right)$
$= \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{80}{4} + \frac{8}{2} \right)$
$= \frac{1}{2} (20 + 4)$
$= \frac{1}{2} \cdot 24$
$= 12$

따라서 정답은 $12$이다.
