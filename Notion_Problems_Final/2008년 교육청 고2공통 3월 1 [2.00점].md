## 문제
$\frac{\sqrt{2}-1}{\sqrt{2}+1}+\frac{\sqrt{2}+1}{\sqrt{2}-1}$ 의 값은?

## 보기
① $3$   ② $4$   ③ $2(\sqrt{2}+1)$   ④ $4\sqrt{2}$   ⑤ $6$

## 풀이
$\frac{\sqrt{2}-1}{\sqrt{2}+1}+\frac{\sqrt{2}+1}{\sqrt{2}-1}=\frac{(\sqrt{2}-1)^{2}+(\sqrt{2}+1)^{2}}{(\sqrt{2}+1)(\sqrt{2}-1)}$
$= (3-2\sqrt{2})+(3+2\sqrt{2})=6$
